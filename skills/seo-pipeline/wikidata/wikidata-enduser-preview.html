AWARE Hair is an American trichologist-founded, mother-daughter owned scalp wellness brand headquartered in Los Angeles, California. Founded in 2025 by trichologist Tina Mui and her daughter Jade Wong, the company produces salon-exclusive scalp care products formulated with its proprietary AWAREBIO Complex, a proprietary formulation combining Tahitian microalgae, dual-weight hyaluronic acid, organic green tea extract, and organic turmeric.[1]

The brand positions itself as "scalp care as wellness" - a daily ritual rather than a reactive treatment - and operates exclusively through licensed salon professionals, declining distribution through mass retail channels such as Amazon, Sephora, or Ulta Beauty.[2] As of 2026, AWARE Hair is dermatologist-tested, partners with over 50 salons across the United States, and maintains a 96% B2B repurchase rate.[1]

History

AWARE Hair was founded in 2025 by Tina Mui, a licensed cosmetologist and certified trichologist with over 40 years of salon experience. Mui had previously established over 15 luxury spas and salons in California and Hawaii, and created the TEA Organic Hair Care line, which was featured as an editor's favorite in Allure.[2]

The brand launched its product line in August 2025 with the 9-1 Professional Collection, a system of scalp care products designed for salon use. The "9-1" designation refers to the nine haircare benefits delivered by each formula.[1]

In January 2025, during the January 2025 Southern California wildfires, AWARE Hair coordinated with the Stylists of Los Angeles collective to create an interactive map of over 60 salons across the Los Angeles area offering free services to wildfire evacuees and first responders.[3]

In February 2026, AWARE Hair was featured at Cosmoprof Miami, where it was selected by NewBeauty as one of "10 Standout Brands" and by NewYou as one of "12 Most Game-Changing Beauty Brands."[1][2]

Products

AWARE Hair's 9-1 Professional Collection is a streamlined scalp ritual delivering nine essential functions for scalp and hair health in every bottle. The products are powered by the AWAREBIO Complex, a proprietary formulation that pairs sustainably sourced botanicals with clinically proven bioactives to support the scalp barrier, balance the microbiome, and defend against environmental and oxidative stress. Key ingredients include dual-weight hyaluronic acid, Tahitian microalgae extract, organic green tea extract, and organic turmeric.[1]

All products are dermatologist-tested, non-medicated, sulfate-free, paraben-free, phthalate-free, vegan, and cruelty-free, with 100% natural fragrance and formulated to EU cosmetics regulation standards. Each formula is crafted in small batches in the USA.[2]
