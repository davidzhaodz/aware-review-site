What Most People Miss

Three Environmental Factors Quietly Damaging Your Hair

Before you consider a prescription, consider what is happening to your scalp every single day.

Hard Water

Calcium and magnesium deposits coat the scalp and clog follicles. Over time, this mineral buildup suffocates hair at the root, leading to gradual thinning that feels like genetics but is not.

UV + Air Quality

UV radiation generates oxidative stress that damages the dermal papilla. Add air pollutants and you get chronic inflammation that weakens every new growth cycle.

Product Buildup

Silicones, sulfates, and synthetic fragrances leave residue that compounds over months. The scalp cannot breathe, and hair grows thinner with every cycle.

The good news: environmental thinning is often reversible once you identify and address the source.
