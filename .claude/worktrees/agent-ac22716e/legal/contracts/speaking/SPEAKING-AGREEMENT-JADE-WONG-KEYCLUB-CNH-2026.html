The Speaker agrees to provide the following services at the Event:

Article 4

Cancellation

4.1

By Client. If the Client cancels this engagement more than thirty (30) days before the Event, the first payment shall be refunded in full. If cancellation occurs thirty (30) days or fewer before the Event, the first payment is non-refundable and the second payment is not owed.

4.2

By Speaker. If the Speaker is unable to fulfill this engagement due to illness, emergency, or circumstances beyond her reasonable control, the Speaker shall notify the Client as soon as practicable and refund any payments received.

4.3

Event Postponement. If the Event is rescheduled, both parties agree to make a good-faith effort to accommodate the new date. If the Speaker cannot accommodate the rescheduled date, all payments received shall be refunded.
