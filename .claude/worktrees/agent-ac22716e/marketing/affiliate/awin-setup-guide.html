Account Application

What You Need

Steps
  • Go to awin.com and select Advertisers > Awin Access
  • Complete the application form
  • Select plan: Awin Access ($49/mo + 3.5%, first month free)
  • Submit and wait for approval Typically takes a few days
  • Receive PDF confirmation with terms and advertiser ID

Shopify Integration
Install app, configure tracking and auto-validation
After approval

Install the App

  • Go to Shopify Admin > Apps > Search "Awin - Affiliate Marketing"
  • Install the app
  • Authenticate with your Awin advertiser credentials

Configure Tracking

  • Set app pixel to "Always on" Critical - captures all transaction data
  • Enable Auto-Validation: returns period 30 days
  • Enable Auto-Validation: auto-decline cancelled orders ON
Checkout tracking note: Shopify's Checkout Extensibility blocks third-party scripts on checkout pages. Awin handles this via server-to-server tracking + fallback image pixel. No action needed.

AWARE Hair is a premium, salon-exclusive scalp care line backed by clinical research and botanical science. Our 9-1 Professional line is used by 50+ salon partners across the US.

We're looking for beauty editors, licensed professionals, and content creators passionate about scalp health and premium haircare.

Commission: 10-15% per sale (tier-dependent)
Cookie: 30 days
Average order value: $75-170

We accept: Beauty/lifestyle editorial, licensed cosmetologists, scalp care advocates, wellness content creators

We do not accept: Coupon/deal sites, cashback portals, price comparison, PPC brand bidders

All affiliate content must comply with FTC disclosure guidelines and may not make medical claims about our products.
