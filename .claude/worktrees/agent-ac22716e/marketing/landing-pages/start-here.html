Your Routine

The 4R Protocol

A simple, four-step system designed by a clinical trichologist. No guesswork, no complicated routines.

Step 1: Reset

9·1 Shampoo

Massage into scalp for 60 seconds. Focus on your scalp, not your hair - this is where the work happens.

Step 2: Repair

9·1 Conditioner

Leave for 3-5 minutes. Apply from mid-length to ends, then work into scalp. The repair happens during the wait.

Step 3: Reinforce

9·1 Leave-In

Apply to towel-dried hair. 1-2 pumps for short hair, 3-4 for long. Protects against environmental damage all day.

Step 4: Reactivate

Gua Sha

Use 3-5x per week with light-medium pressure to boost circulation and release tension. 2-3 minutes across your entire scalp.

How long until I see results?

Most clients notice improvements in scalp comfort within 2-4 weeks. Visible hair improvements typically appear between 8-12 weeks of consistent use. The 4R Protocol works with your scalp's natural renewal cycle, so patience is rewarded.

Is AWARE suitable for color-treated hair?

Yes. All AWARE products are pH-balanced and formulated to be safe for color-treated hair. Our gentle formulas help maintain color vibrancy while improving scalp health. Many colorists in our partner salons use AWARE on freshly colored clients.

Are your products sulfate-free?

Yes, all AWARE products are sulfate-free, paraben-free, and formulated without harsh chemicals. We use coconut-derived surfactants that create a rich, satisfying lather without stripping natural oils or damaging the scalp barrier.
