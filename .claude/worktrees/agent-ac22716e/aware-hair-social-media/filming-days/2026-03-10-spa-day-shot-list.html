"The Scalp Facial" Ritual Demo
Tina leads IG Reel + TikTok 30-45 sec Scalp Science

  4. Gua Sha massage on scalp - slow, deliberate strokes

After 40+ years behind the chair, this is the scalp ritual I recommend to every client: double cleanse to purify, mask to restore, massage to stimulate circulation, and daily protection from UV and pollution. This isn't a trend. It's basic skin science applied where it matters most. #scalpcare #scalphealth #professionalhaircare #scalpenvironment #salonlife
