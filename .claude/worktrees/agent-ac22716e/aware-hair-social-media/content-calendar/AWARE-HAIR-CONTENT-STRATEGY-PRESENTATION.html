THE 5 CONTENT PILLARS

Pillar Breakdown

1. SCALP SCIENCE EDUCATION (30%)

Purpose: Position as THE authority on scalp health Content Themes: - Scalp as skin (microbiome, barrier health) - Ingredient deep-dives for scalp health - Science made simple - Myth-busting (sulfates, silicones, “clean” claims)

Why it works: Differentiates from “just another hair brand”

2. FOUNDER STORY & VULNERABILITY (25%)

Purpose: Build emotional connection through authentic storytelling Content Themes: - Your mom’s 40-year salon journey - Your personal hair loss/scalp struggles - Why you left consulting for shampoo - The hard middle parts (not just wins) - Business decisions explained transparently

Why it works: Humans buy from humans, not brands

3. RADICAL TRANSPARENCY (20%)

Purpose: Justify premium pricing through education Content Themes: - Behind-the-scenes manufacturing - “Why we don’t” contrarian series - Price justification/ingredient cost breakdowns - What makes 9-in-1 actually different - Small-batch California manufacturing

Why it works: Transparency builds trust, trust builds loyalty

4. COMMUNITY & RESULTS (15%)

Purpose: Social proof and customer celebration Content Themes: - Customer transformation stories - Before/after scalp/hair health - UGC features - Testimonial highlights - Interactive Q&As

Why it works: UGC has 4x higher CTR than brand content

5. LIFESTYLE & RELATABILITY (10%)

Purpose: Entertainment and trend participation Content Themes: - “Signs your mom is a hairdresser” - Day-in-the-life founder content - Relatable beauty struggles - Trending audio participation with products

Why it works: Balance education with entertainment

CONTENT CONCEPTS

(Bullet Point Format Only - No Full Scripts)

Scalp First Science Series

Concept 1: “Your Scalp is Skin”

  • Hook: “You treat your face like skin. Why don’t you treat your scalp the same?”
  • Compare face routine to scalp routine (visual side-by-side)
  • Same structure, barrier, microbiome, needs
  • More complex: 100,000 follicles embedded
  • Why harsh sulfates/synthetic fragrance wrong
  • Paradigm shift: treat scalp like luxury skincare
  • CTA: Follow for scalp science series

Concept 2: “3 Signs Your Scalp Barrier is Broken”

  • Hook: “If you have any of these 3 signs, your scalp is screaming for help”
  • Sign 1: Visible scalp through hair (barrier breakdown)
  • Sign 2: Waxy/rough after washing (buildup suffocating follicles)
  • Sign 3: Oily roots, dry ends (overcompensating scalp)
  • Explain: scalp has microbiome like gut
  • Solution: fix scalp barrier first, hair follows
  • CTA: Download free Scalp Health Guide

Concept 3: “Why Your Shampoo Isn’t Actually Cleaning”

  • Hook: “Your shampoo isn’t cleaning your scalp. Here’s why…”
  • Most clean hair, not scalp
  • Scalp = skin with pores, barrier, microbiome
  • Harsh sulfates strip everything (good + bad)
  • Scalp panics, overproduces oil, cycle continues
  • Our approach: cleanse deeply WITHOUT stripping
  • Real customer quote: “could see scalp → thick hair”
  • CTA: Learn the science - link in bio

Why We Don’t Series

Concept 1: “Why We’ll Never Discount”

  • Hook: “People ask why we’ll never discount. Here’s the truth…”
  • 6 years of development
  • Fragrance alone costs 20x more
  • Dual-weight HA (luxury skincare tech)
  • Tahitian microalgae from Pacific
  • Small-batch California manufacturing
  • Discounting = either overpriced OR cutting corners
  • Salon partners: discounting undermines their professional pricing
  • Customer result: “could see scalp → thick hair”
  • CTA: Real results from first use

Concept 2: “Why Natural Fragrance Costs 20x More”

  • Hook: “Our fragrance costs 20 times more. Here’s why we did it anyway…”
  • Choice: synthetic (cheap) vs 100% natural (20x cost)
  • Everyone said we were crazy
  • Synthetic fragrances = scalp irritation, microbiome disruption
  • Spent months with master perfumers
  • Pacific Air: aromatic botanicals + grounding woods
  • Functional aromatherapy (every ingredient promotes scalp vitality)
  • Did it almost bankrupt us? Yes.
  • Customer: “best smelling clean haircare”
  • CTA: Experience Pacific Air

KEY PRINCIPLES

The Content Philosophy

1. Authenticity Over Perfection

Raw, unfiltered content builds more trust than polished ads. Action: Don’t overthink production—focus on message clarity.

2. Education Before Promotion

Provide value 80% of the time, promote 20% of the time. Action: Every video should teach or connect before asking for sale.

3. Series Create Anticipation

Recurring content trains viewers to return and builds loyalty. Action: Stick to series schedule even when tempted to post trends.

4. Founder Vulnerability Wins

Your authentic story is your competitive advantage. Action: Share struggles, not just successes. The middle matters.

5. First 3 Seconds Decide Everything

65% of viewers decide to continue within 1.5 seconds. Action: Hook with problem, controversy, or relatable pain point.

The 80/20 Content Rule

80% Value Content: - Tutorials and how-tos - Ingredient education - Myth-busting - Behind-the-scenes transparency - Founder vulnerability stories - Customer transformations

20% Promotional Content: - Product launches - Sales and offers - Direct CTAs - “Shop now” messaging
