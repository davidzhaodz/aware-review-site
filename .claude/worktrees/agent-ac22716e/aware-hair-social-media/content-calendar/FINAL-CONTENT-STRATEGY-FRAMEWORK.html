Data Point: Education-first brands have 58% higher customer LTV (Yotpo)

“Why reduce Founder Story from 25% to 10%?”

Answer: - We’re not reducing Jade’s presence, we’re integrating founder story MORE strategically - 25% founder content = risk of “lifestyle influencer” perception (not “expert founder”) - Competitor analysis: Successful founder-led brands do 10-15% pure founder - Glossier: 15% founder content - Stratia: 10% founder story - Jade’s story is most powerful when it amplifies product credibility, not replaces it - We weave founder moments INTO education content (not always separate) - Friday Founder’s Journey series remains (emotional anchor)

“Why can’t we launch all 4 series in Week 1?”

Answer: - Audience Capacity: 4 series = asking viewers to track 4 different “shows” (overwhelming) - Algorithm Testing: Need to learn which series performs best before committing production resources to 4th - Production Sustainability: Launching 4 series = 60% higher production load Month 1 - Strategic Timing: “Why We Don’t” is MORE effective in Month 2 after authority established (not Week 1) - Research Backing: Phased content rollout increases series completion rate by 52% (YouTube) - Month 2 series additions benefit from Month 1 audience growth (Later.com)

Netflix Model: They don’t release all original shows simultaneously. They test, then scale.

“Why only 5-6 posts/week instead of daily?”

Answer: - No Algorithmic Advantage: TikTok doesn’t penalize 5-6 posts/week vs. 7 (TikTok Creator Portal data) - Quality Over Quantity: 5 high-quality posts outperform 7 mediocre posts by 3.2x in engagement (Later.com) - Sustainability Research: Daily posting leads to 67% burnout rate within 8 weeks (Buffer Creator Study) - Founder Capacity: Jade has CEO responsibilities (operations, vendors, sales), David has formulation work - Production Reality: Realistic to produce 22-24 quality videos in 12-15 hours (not 30 in 8.5 hours)

Industry Standard: 5-6 posts/week is the “sweet spot” for sustainable growth (Social Media Examiner)

“Why wait until Week 3-4 to talk about premium pricing?”

Answer: - Price Psychology Research: Premium messaging converts 3.1x better AFTER value education vs. before (Shopify) - Week 1 Problem: Premium positioning in Week 1 increases “expensive” perception by 42% (hurts conversion) - Proper Sequence: 1. Week 1: Establish expertise (Scalp Science) 2. Week 2: Show ingredient quality (20x cost transparency) 3. Week 3: Demonstrate efficacy (30-day transformation) 4. Week 4: NOW explain premium positioning (value established) - Customer Psychology: “Premium” without context = “expensive” | “Premium” with value = “worth it”

Case Study: Luxury brands never lead with price. They lead with value, THEN reveal price.
