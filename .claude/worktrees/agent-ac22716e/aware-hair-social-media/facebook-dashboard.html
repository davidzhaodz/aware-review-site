Decision Triggers

When to scale, maintain, pause, or pivot.

Scale
Metric beats target by 20%+ for 2 consecutive weeks. Winning creative CPA under 50% of threshold. Increase budget 20% every 3-4 days.
Maintain
Metric within 10% of target. CPA at threshold. Engagement rate 2-4%. Continue current approach, no changes needed.
Pause
Metric misses target by 30%+ for 2 weeks. CPA exceeds 2x threshold for 3+ days. CTR drops below 0.5%. Pause and analyze.
Pivot
Metric misses by 50%+. Declining trend over 3+ weeks. Hook angle consistently underperforms. Significant strategy change needed.
