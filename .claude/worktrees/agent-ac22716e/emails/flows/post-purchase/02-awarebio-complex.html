Two molecular weights work together - low-weight HA penetrates the scalp for deep hydration, while high-weight HA forms a protective moisture barrier on the hair strand.

Rich in essential lipids, peptides, and biotics that nourish the scalp microbiome and help reduce hair fall for healthier, fuller hair.
