Scalp Insight

Most scalp issues don't start as "issues" - they start as small signals you ignore

Reactive scalpcare is what most of us do by default: we only pay attention when the scalp starts signaling problems.

Itch - "What can I use to stop this?"

Flakes - "I need something medicated."

Oil - "I'm not cleansing enough."

Shedding - "I need to switch everything."

The problem is: by the time you feel symptoms, your scalp has often been quietly struggling for a while. That's why someone can be oily and flaky at the same time, or why flakes can worsen after "deep cleaning." It isn't always a cleanliness problem - it's often a stability problem.

Proactive scalpcare = fewer flare-ups

Instead of "attacking" the symptom, you focus on consistent, gentle cleansing, barrier comfort, and supporting balance over time. This is the philosophy AWARE was built around.

The "60-60 Rule"

"Spend 60 seconds massaging shampoo into the scalp before adding more water, then 60 seconds rinsing thoroughly."

Most people rush both, leaving either buildup behind or over-scrubbing to compensate. This is one of the simplest "proactive" habits we teach in AWARE education.
