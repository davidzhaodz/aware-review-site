04 / 08
AI Engine Optimization

Optimized for the next search engine.
Not just Google.

Every article is built to be extractable by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. We call this AEO: Answer Engine Optimization. It's the difference between being indexed and being cited.

◆
Inverted Pyramid
Every article opens with a direct answer in the first two sentences. AI engines extract lead paragraphs first. Ours are written to be the citation.
◇
Self-Contained Tables
Comparison tables that make sense when extracted from the page. AI engines pull tables verbatim. Ours include the context they need to stand alone.
△
Entity Reinforcement
"AWARE Hair" appears 5+ times. "AWAREBIO Complex" is defined inline. "Tina Mui, Certified Trichologist" is cited with credentials. AI needs repeated entity signals.
□
FAQPage Schema
Structured data matching visible FAQ sections. Google's AI Overview and Perplexity both consume FAQPage JSON-LD. Every article includes 6+ Q&A pairs.
○
External Authority
2-3 citations per article from NIH, AAD, USGS. AI engines weigh content that cites the same sources they trust. Our references are strategic, not decorative.
◊
Key Takeaway Boxes
Extractable summary after each section. Structured as "Key Takeaway: [insight]." Designed for AI snippet extraction and featured snippet candidacy.
