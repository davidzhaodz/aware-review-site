From the perspective of a skeptical distributor brand committee evaluating AWARE.

1. "$55-75 is a tough sell for an unknown brand."

Most professional products sit $28-42 retail. AWARE is 30-60% above that without name recognition.
Counter: 96% repurchase proves salons reorder after first buy. 50% salon margin at $27.50 wholesale is same or better than most lines. Price works because clients are already seeking scalp care (18M+ organic views = inbound demand).

2. "50 salons isn't enough to prove anything."

50 salons in one metro doesn't prove regional or national sell-through.
Counter: Acknowledge directly: "We're early, that's why we want a distribution partner." 96% repurchase over 50 salons is a stronger signal than 200 salons at 60% repurchase. Frame as product-market fit proof, not scale proof.

3. "We just onboarded new brands. Reps are maxed."

Onboarding costs 3-6 months of rep education time. New brand fatigue is real.
Counter: Only 3-5 SKUs, not a 20-SKU line. It's a "category add" not a "brand replacement." Scalp care doesn't compete with color, styling, or treatment lines - it's additive revenue.

4. "Scalp care is niche. Is there enough demand?"

Scalp care is still emerging in professional channel. Most distributors haven't seen sustained demand.
Counter: 18M+ organic social views proves consumer demand. Head spa trend going mainstream. Key insight: salons offering scalp services see higher spend on color and styling too - lifts the whole ticket.

5. "You're LA-only. Can you support my territory?"

Distributors need brands that show up for in-salon training, events, issue resolution.
Counter: Made in LA, domestic fulfillment, 4-week lead time. Virtual training for distant markets + in-person for key launches. $5,100 Starter Package is a low-risk test. Distributor is the local expert - AWARE provides product + content.

6. "No brand awareness in my market."

Unlike Olaplex or K18, nobody outside SoCal has heard of AWARE.
Counter: 18M+ organic views are national. AWARE's Scalp Environment Report content generates inbound interest. Sample kits (1 per 6 retail units) so reps lead with product experience, not brand pitch.

7. "What if the founder loses interest or brand folds?"

Small founder-led brands are a real risk. Distributors have been burned before.
Counter: $125K revenue in year one, 96% repurchase, raising capital ($1-3M), patent-pending, GMP/MoCRA compliant. Offer to share sell-through data transparently - openness builds trust.
