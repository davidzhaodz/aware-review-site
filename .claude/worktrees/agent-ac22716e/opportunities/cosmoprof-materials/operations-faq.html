Ordering & Fulfillment

What is the minimum opening order?
$5,000 for initial distributor orders. This can be a pre-built package or custom mix of case packs.
What is the reorder minimum?
$2,500 per order. Mix and match by case pack to meet minimum.
What is the lead time?
4 weeks from PO confirmation. Rush orders may be available - contact us to discuss.
How are products packaged?
All products ship in 6-unit case packs. Cases are shelf-ready with individual UPC codes on each unit.
Where do you ship from?
Los Angeles, CA. All products manufactured and fulfilled domestically.
What are the payment terms?
Net 30 available upon credit approval. Prepay options also available with 2% discount.

Pricing & Territory

Is MAP pricing enforced?
Yes. Minimum advertised prices are strictly enforced to protect margins at all levels. MAP = Retail pricing.
Is territory exclusivity available?
Yes, for qualified partners with $50K+ annual commitment. All territories currently open for discussion.
Are products sold on Amazon or DTC?
No Amazon, no Sephora, no mass retail. AWARE is salon-exclusive. DTC is limited to brand website only with salon-matching pricing.

Supply & Scalability

Can you scale to 500+ salon doors?
Yes. Manufacturing capacity and supply chain are prepared for significant growth. We've built infrastructure for scale.
Where are products manufactured?
Los Angeles, California. All formulation and production is domestic, USA-made.
What is the shelf life?
24 months unopened from date of manufacture. Products ship with minimum 18 months remaining.
What is your return policy?
Damaged or defective products replaced at no charge. We have a 0% quality return rate across all accounts to date.

Marketing & Support

Is co-op marketing available?
Yes. Marketing budget available for qualified partners. Discuss specific programs during partnership conversation.
What training materials are provided?
Comprehensive sales training for your reps, plus salon education materials. In-salon training support available.
Do you provide POS displays?
Yes. Display units and merchandising materials included with opening orders. Reorder as needed.
What about salon samples?
Sample kits available for salon prospecting. Policy: 1 sample kit per 6 retail units ordered.
