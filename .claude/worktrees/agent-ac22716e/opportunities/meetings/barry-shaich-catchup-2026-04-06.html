The Direct Asks

This is a catchup, but the #1 goal is concrete: who does Barry know, and what can he actually do for AWARE?

Ask #1: Map His Network

"Barry, I want to be direct. You've been in this industry 30 years. You know people we don't know yet. Who in your network should AWARE be talking to right now?"

  • Ward Bassett - this was the target from meeting #1. Is this intro still on the table? What happened?
  • Other distributors - who else does he have warm relationships with? Regional? Specialty?
  • Retailers - beyond Bigelow, does he know buyers at other prestige retail?
  • Press/media - any beauty editor or trade publication contacts?

Goal: Walk away with 2-3 named people he's willing to introduce you to, with a timeline.

Ask #2: ABS Chicago (April 18-20)

"You're going to ABS - what does your schedule look like there? Can we coordinate?"

  • Is AWARE attending? If not, can Barry be eyes/ears on the ground?
  • Who is he meeting with at ABS that might be relevant to AWARE?
  • Can he facilitate any introductions at the show?
  • Distributors known to attend ABS: PSC, Beauty Craft, Mid America Beauty, Premier

Goal: Either get specific ABS intros scheduled, or get an honest read on whether that's realistic.

Ask #3: Define the Working Relationship

"What does a working relationship between us actually look like? I don't want to waste your time or mine."

  • Is he looking for a referral fee on closed deals? A consulting arrangement? Equity? Goodwill?
  • AWARE's position: happy to pay referral fees on closed distribution deals, but not a retainer for introductions that haven't materialized
  • Be honest about where you are: "We've got several paths opening up. I want to know if you're one of them."

Goal: Clear terms, or a clear decision to part ways respectfully.

Watch For

Signals He's Genuinely Useful
  • Names specific people, not categories ("I know the buyer at X" vs. "I know people in distribution")
  • Offers to make introductions on a specific timeline
  • Has tried the products and speaks about them with real enthusiasm
  • Asks smart questions about AWARE's current salon performance
Red Flags
  • Pivots to pitching EBL services (DTC, Amazon, TikTok Shop) - redirect to B2B connector role
  • Vague about his contacts ("I know everyone in the industry" with no names)
  • Hasn't tried the products or can't speak to them specifically
  • Asks about equity or formal advisory role before delivering any value
  • Ward Bassett intro has evaporated with no explanation
