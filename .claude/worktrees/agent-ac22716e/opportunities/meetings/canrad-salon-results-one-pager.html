Where We're Succeeding

Across Market Tiers

Our strongest results come from a range of salon environments, not just ultra-luxury:

  • Upper-mainstream salons - Owner-operated, everyday clientele. Consistent reorders and strong sell-through.
  • High-end salons - Premium positioning validates the brand and attracts clientele who already invest in wellness.
  • Suite professionals - Independent stylists retailing from their own chair. Not celebrity stylists.
  • Affiliate partners - Salons that recommend via our affiliate program, expanding reach without stocking inventory.

The $60 Haircut Threshold

Our data shows a clear pattern: salons charging $60+ per haircut can successfully retail AWARE products. That's not ultra-luxury - that's a common price point across thousands of quality salons in North America.

The key is not the neighborhood. It's having an owner or lead stylist who champions the product and understands the scalp care conversation.

AWARE is preventative scalp care, not reactive treatment. Clients aren't buying it because they have a problem - they're buying it to maintain scalp health, the same way they invest in skincare. This positions it alongside facial serums and wellness products, not alongside $15 drugstore shampoo. The competitor isn't another shampoo - it's the client's skincare budget.
