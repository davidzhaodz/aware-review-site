Step-by-Step Protocol

What Your Therapist Does

1
Purifying Cleanse 10 min
Remove environmental buildup, product residue, and excess sebum. Warm water, gentle scalp massage with fingertips in circular motions.
Product: 9·1 Professional Scalp Care Shampoo (pH-balanced, color-safe, sulfate-free)
2
Nourishing Scalp Mask 15-20 min
Apply Nourishing Conditioner/Mask generously to scalp in sections. Process under warm towel or hooded dryer. Heat helps the AWAREBIO Complex penetrate for deeper barrier repair and microbiome support.
Product: 9·1 Professional Scalp Care Conditioner (hyaluronic acid, microbiome support)
3
Gua Sha Meridian Massage 10-15 min
Stainless Steel Gua Sha with light-medium pressure along meridian lines. Can be performed on wet or dry hair. Stimulates circulation, relieves tension, promotes product absorption. Pairs beautifully with our upcoming treatment oil and serum. The signature moment of the treatment.
Tool: Stainless Steel Gua Sha
4
Seal + Protect 5 min
Apply Leave-In Treatment as a finishing shield. Gel-serum hybrid that seals in moisture, smooths frizz, provides 360-degree environmental defense (heat, UV, pollution), and holds light style memory. Recommended with a blowout. The step clients notice most.
Product: Leave-In Treatment (UV + environmental shield)
