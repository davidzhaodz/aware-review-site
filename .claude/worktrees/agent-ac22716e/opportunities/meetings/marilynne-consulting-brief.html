Part III
PR & Awards Strategy
Where We Are

No PR Firm, Self-Submitted Awards

We have no PR firm. We self-submitted to Marie Claire Beauty Awards ($900, 3 entries) and CEW Indie Hair Award ($1,000). All press has been organic or self-generated. We don't have a systematic pitching engine.

Why This Matters

Your second Allure Best of Beauty seal drove a 527% increase in year-over-year sales - the kind of outcome that could transform AWARE's trajectory. We want to understand how to build that PR engine.

What We Need

PR Support Areas

  • PR firm evaluation - How should we hire? What's a reasonable budget at our stage? What questions should we ask?
  • Awards strategy - Which awards to target beyond Allure, Marie Claire, CEW? Submission timing and process?
  • Pre-firm momentum - Can we build press momentum before affording a full PR firm? Direct editor outreach?
  • Pitching cadence - You mentioned weekly pitching. How does a founder-led effort work before we can afford a firm?
  • Affiliate link model - How PR drove retail sales through affiliate links. Could we adapt this for DTC?

Tina does all education in-person. We have no virtual content, no pre-recorded videos, no brand education kit for distributors. Based on our conversation, this is a gap we need to close before any distribution deal.

What We Need
