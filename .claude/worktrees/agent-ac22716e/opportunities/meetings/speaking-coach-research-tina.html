What to Avoid
Not the Right Fit
Toastmasters / generic public speaking groups: The issue isn't fear of speaking - it's the absence of executive communication training. Group practice won't provide the direct, uncomfortable feedback Tina needs.

Beauty business coaches: They coach salon owners on business-building, not executive communication in B2B/investor settings. Completely different skill.

Anxiety-focused coaches: Tina isn't anxious about speaking. She's too comfortable speaking. The issue is structure and discipline, not fear.

One-off workshop formats: 40 years of communication habits won't change in a weekend. Minimum 6-session engagement, ideally 3-6 months with a primary coach.

Women's empowerment / thought leadership coaches: The goal isn't to amplify Tina's voice - it's to focus it. She needs less volume, more precision.
