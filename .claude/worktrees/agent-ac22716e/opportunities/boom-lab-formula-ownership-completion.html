Formula Ownership Confirmation
& Assignment Agreement

Effective Date: October 8, 2025  |  Execution Date: April __, 2026

This Formula Ownership Confirmation & Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Execution Date set forth above, with an Effective Date of October 8, 2025, by and between:

AWARE Hair LLC, a California limited liability company, with its principal office located at 153 E Holly Street, Pasadena, CA 91103 ("Company"),

and

Boom Lab Inc, a California corporation (Corporation Number C4285797), with its principal office located at 4592 Green Tree Ln, Irvine, CA 92612 ("Developer").

Company and Developer are each referred to individually as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

Recitals

WHEREAS, Company and Developer are parties to a Manufacturing Agreement dated July 22, 2024 ("Manufacturing Agreement"), as amended October 2024 and March 27, 2026, under which Developer formulated three (3) proprietary product formulations for Company (collectively, the "Formulas");

WHEREAS, Developer issued Invoice No. 9262025-4 dated September 26, 2025 for $7,500.00 ($2,500.00 per Formula) to transfer ownership of all three Formulas, of which Company has paid $5,000.00, with the final $2,500.00 due upon execution of this Agreement;

WHEREAS, Developer has delivered all Formula documentation to Company (final delivery April 10, 2026), and the Manufacturing Agreement may contain provisions relating to intellectual property ownership or formula rights ("Prior IP Provisions") that the Parties wish this Agreement to supersede;

WHEREAS, the Parties wish to confirm the assignment of all right, title, and interest in the Formulas to Company and memorialize the terms of such transfer;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

Article 1

Formulas

1.1

Defined Formulas. The "Formulas" subject to this Agreement consist of the following three (3) product formulations developed by Developer for Company:

Product Formula Reference Ownership Fee
AWARE 9·1 Professional Shampoo Per original development documentation $2,500.00
AWARE 9·1 Professional Conditioner Per original development documentation $2,500.00
AWARE 9·1 Professional Leave-In Styling Conditioner Per original development documentation $2,500.00
Total Formula Ownership Fee $7,500.00
1.2

Scope of Formulas. Each Formula includes, without limitation: the complete ingredient composition and proportions, active ingredient concentrations, manufacturing batch procedures, mixing sequences, processing parameters, pH and viscosity targets, raw ingredient supplier names and contact information, and all related technical documentation, notes, and records created by Developer in connection with the development of such Formula. "Formulas" also includes any modifications, reformulations, iterations, or improvements to the foregoing developed by Developer for Company, whether at Company's direction or Developer's recommendation.

Article 2

Payment Confirmation

2.1

Payment History. Company has made the following payments to Developer toward Invoice No. 9262025-4:

# Date Amount Reference

Total Paid $7,500.00 Invoice No. 9262025-4 - Paid in Full
2.2

Payment Reconciliation. The Parties acknowledge and agree that Payments No. 1 and No. 2, totaling Five Thousand Dollars ($5,000.00), were applied toward Invoice No. 9262025-4 for formula ownership.

2.3

Full Settlement. Company shall remit Payment No. 3 ($2,500.00) within five (5) business days of execution of this Agreement. Upon receipt of such payment, Invoice No. 9262025-4 shall be deemed paid in full, and Developer shall have no further claim for compensation related to Formula ownership, development, or transfer.

Article 3

Assignment of Rights

3.1

Assignment. Developer hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Company all of Developer's right, title, and interest in and to the Formulas, including without limitation all intellectual property rights, trade secrets, know-how, and proprietary information embodied in or related to the Formulas. This assignment is effective as of October 8, 2025 (the "Effective Date"). To the extent any Formula was delivered to Company after the Effective Date, this assignment applies to the final version of such Formula as delivered.

3.2

Scope of Assignment. The rights assigned hereunder include, without limitation:

  • All ingredient compositions, percentages, and concentrations
  • All manufacturing processes, batch procedures, and processing parameters
  • All technical documentation, batch records, formulation notes, and development records
  • All trade secrets and proprietary know-how related to the Formulas
  • The right to use, modify, adapt, improve, and create derivative formulations
  • The right to have the Formulas manufactured by any third party
  • The right to sublicense any or all rights in the Formulas
  • The right to seek intellectual property protection, including patents and trade secret protection, in any jurisdiction worldwide
3.3

Future Modifications. Any modifications, reformulations, or improvements to the Formulas developed by Developer for Company under the Manufacturing Agreement shall be deemed part of the Formulas and automatically owned by Company upon creation, without additional formula ownership fees. This does not affect Developer's right to charge standard R&D, batch sampling, or testing fees for such work as agreed under the Manufacturing Agreement.

3.4

Perpetual and Worldwide. The assignment granted under this Article 3 is perpetual, irrevocable, and worldwide in scope. No further consideration beyond the payments set forth in Article 2 shall be required.

Article 4

Exclusivity & Representations

4.1

Developer Representations. Developer represents and warrants that:

  • Developer is the formulator and developer of each Formula;
  • Developer has full right, power, and authority to assign the Formulas as set forth herein;
  • The Formulas have not been previously assigned, licensed, pledged, or encumbered in any way;
  • No third party has any claim, right, or interest in the Formulas;
  • The Formulas do not, to Developer's knowledge, infringe upon the intellectual property rights of any third party.
4.2

Non-Use and Non-Disclosure. Developer agrees that, from the Effective Date forward, Developer shall not directly or indirectly use, manufacture, sell, license, or disclose any of the Formulas to any third party, except as expressly permitted under Section 4.3.

4.3

Manufacturing Exception. Notwithstanding Section 4.2, Developer retains the right to possess and use the Formulas solely for the purpose of manufacturing products for Company under the Manufacturing Agreement (as amended). This exception terminates upon expiration or termination of the Manufacturing Agreement.

4.4

Return of Materials. Upon expiration or termination of the Manufacturing Agreement, Developer shall promptly return or destroy all copies of the Formulas and related documentation in Developer's possession, and shall certify in writing that it has done so.

4.5

Further Assurances. Developer shall, at Company's request and expense, execute and deliver any additional documents, instruments, or agreements reasonably necessary to perfect, confirm, or evidence the assignment of rights set forth in this Agreement, including without limitation declarations, assignments, or filings related to intellectual property protection.

Article 5

Confidentiality

5.1

Confidential Information. The Formulas and all related documentation constitute Company's confidential information and trade secrets. Developer shall maintain the strict confidentiality of the Formulas and shall not disclose any Formula or any component thereof to any third party, except as required for manufacturing under the Manufacturing Agreement or as required by law.

5.2

Survival. The confidentiality obligations set forth in this Article 5 shall survive the expiration or termination of this Agreement and the Manufacturing Agreement indefinitely.

Article 6

General Provisions

6.1

Mutual Release. Upon full payment as set forth in Article 2, the Parties mutually release each other from any and all claims, demands, or obligations arising from or related to Invoice No. 9262025-4 and the formula ownership transfer. This release does not affect any rights or obligations under the Manufacturing Agreement.

6.2

Relationship to Manufacturing Agreement. This Agreement pertains solely to the ownership and assignment of the Formulas. The Manufacturing Agreement dated July 22, 2024, as amended by the October 2024 Addendum and the March 27, 2026 Amendment, remains in full force and effect except that any provisions therein relating to intellectual property ownership, formula rights, or work product ownership are superseded by this Agreement (see Section 6.5).

6.3

Indemnification. Developer shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Company from and against any claims, damages, losses, or expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from any breach of Developer's representations and warranties set forth in Article 4.

6.4

Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflicts of law provisions.

6.5

Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter of formula ownership and assignment, and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements relating thereto, including Invoice No. 9262025-4 (which is hereby satisfied in full) and any provisions of the Manufacturing Agreement, its Addendum, or its Amendment relating to intellectual property ownership, formula rights, or work product ownership (the "Prior IP Provisions"). To the extent any Prior IP Provisions conflict with the terms of this Agreement, this Agreement shall control. Except as expressly superseded by the preceding sentence, the Manufacturing Agreement and its amendments remain in full force and effect.

6.6

Counterparts. This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument. Electronic signatures shall be deemed original signatures for all purposes.

6.7

Successors and Assigns. This Agreement shall be binding upon and inure to the benefit of the Parties and their respective successors, assigns, heirs, and legal representatives. Developer may not assign this Agreement or any of its obligations hereunder without the prior written consent of Company. Company may freely assign this Agreement.

6.8

Severability. If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.
