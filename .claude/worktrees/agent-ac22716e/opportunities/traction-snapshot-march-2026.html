The Business

What We Do

AWARE Hair makes science-backed scalp care products sold exclusively through professional salons. Three core SKUs (shampoo, conditioner, leave-in treatment) formulated with botanical active ingredients for scalp health conditions including dryness, sensitivity, and thinning.

Why It Works

The professional channel creates a trust moat. When a stylist recommends AWARE, it carries clinical authority that DTC brands can't replicate. Our Scalp Environment Report (live, generating leads) gives consumers a personalized reason to seek out an AWARE salon.

Where We Are

~50 salon partners across the US, primarily LA/OC metro. Revenue growing organically with zero paid acquisition. Three active distribution conversations (Canada, Western US, strategic advisory) that could accelerate to 150+ salons.

Where We're Going

100 salons by end of 2026 via direct acquisition + selective distribution. $500K run rate by mid-2027. First sales hire in Q3 2026. DTC email list building through the Scalp Environment Report to create consumer-driven salon pull.

Why Now: The Scalp Care Moment

Scalp care is the fastest-growing segment in professional haircare (~7% CAGR). True Beauty Ventures invested in Jupiter (scalp care). K18 was acquired by Unilever for ~$1B. Act+Acre's salon-adjacent scalp positioning grew retail revenue 250% in 2023. The category is being validated by capital and consumers simultaneously - but no brand owns the professional-exclusive scalp care position that AWARE occupies.
