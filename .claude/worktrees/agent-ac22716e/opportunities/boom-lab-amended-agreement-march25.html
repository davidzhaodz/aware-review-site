Legal Document

Amendment to Manufacturing Agreement

March 27, 2026

This Amendment ("Amendment") to the Manufacturing Agreement dated July 22, 2024 ("Original Agreement") is entered into as of March 27, 2026, by and between:

AWARE Hair LLC, a company organized and existing under the laws of the State of California, with its principal office located at 153 E Holly Street, Pasadena, CA 91103 ("Company"),

and

Boom Lab Inc, a company organized and existing under the laws of the State of California, with its principal office located at 4592 Green Tree Ln, Irvine, CA 92612, California Corporation Number C4285797 ("Manufacturer").

Recitals

WHEREAS, Company and Manufacturer entered into the Original Agreement for the manufacture, filling, and packaging of hair care products;

WHEREAS, Manufacturer has proposed revised per-unit pricing to reflect increased material and production costs;

WHEREAS, Company has agreed to accept revised pricing and to assume responsibility for fragrance costs and packaging materials, subject to the terms set forth herein;

WHEREAS, Company has made total payments of $43,890.00 under the Original Agreement and its October 2024 Addendum;

WHEREAS, Manufacturer has delivered 7,400 of the originally contracted 30,000 retail units, with 400 shampoo units from Production Run 1 remaining undelivered;

WHEREAS, the initial one-year term of the Original Agreement expired on or about July 22, 2025, and both parties have continued to operate under its terms in good faith;

WHEREAS, the parties wish to renew the Original Agreement, formalize revised terms for future production, and preserve the Company's accrued credits;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth herein, the parties agree as follows:

Article 1

Scope of Production

1.1

This Amendment covers the production of 22,500 new retail units, allocated as 7,500 units per SKU, across the following products:

  • AWARE 9.1 Professional Shampoo 300ML - 7,500 units
  • AWARE 9.1 Professional Conditioner 300ML - 7,500 units
  • AWARE 9.1 Professional Leave-In Styling Conditioner 250ML - 7,500 units
1.2

Flexible SKU Allocation. While the target allocation is 7,500 units per SKU, the allocation of units across SKUs for individual Purchase Orders shall be at Company's discretion (see Article 7), provided that the aggregate quantity across all Purchase Orders meets or exceeds 22,500 units.

1.3

Production Timeline and Guarantee. Company guarantees ordering and producing a minimum of 15,000 units (total across all SKUs) by September 26, 2027. The remaining units (up to 7,500) may be ordered and produced after September 26, 2027. For clarity, the September 26, 2027 date is a price validity deadline (see Article 2.4), not a production completion deadline for the full 22,500-unit commitment.

1.4

Part Shipments. Production shall be organized via mutually agreed Purchase Orders (see Article 7). Part shipments are allowed, provided that the quantity and timing of each shipment are pre-approved by both Buyer and Seller. There is no fixed minimum order quantity per production run.

1.5

Supersession of Original Volume Commitment. This Amendment supersedes the original production volume commitment of 30,000 retail units under the Original Agreement. The parties' obligations with respect to future production quantities are solely as set forth in this Amendment, for a total of 22,500 new units. Neither party shall have any further obligation to produce, order, or accept units beyond the scope defined herein, except as may be agreed in a subsequent written amendment.

Article 2

Pricing

2.1

The per-unit prices for Products under this Amendment shall be:

Product Price Per Unit
AWARE 9.1 Professional Shampoo 300ML $3.85
AWARE 9.1 Professional Conditioner 300ML $3.25
AWARE 9.1 Professional Leave-In Styling Conditioner 250ML $3.65
2.2

These prices supersede the pricing in Section 5.1 of the Original Agreement for all production under this Amendment.

2.3

Total production value under this Amendment, at the target allocation of 7,500 units per SKU (22,500 total), is $80,625.00, calculated as follows: Shampoo $28,875.00 + Conditioner $24,375.00 + Leave-In $27,375.00. Actual totals shall be determined by the SKU mix ordered in each Purchase Order.

2.4

Price Validity. The prices set forth in Section 2.1 are valid through September 26, 2027. While Pro-Invoice #32620261 dated March 26, 2026 states a price validity of March 26, 2027, the parties hereby agree to extend the price guarantee by six months to September 26, 2027. Units ordered and invoiced on or before September 26, 2027 shall be at the prices in Section 2.1. Pricing for any units ordered after September 26, 2027 shall be subject to mutual renegotiation per Section 13.4 of the Original Agreement.

2.5

Governing Price Document. Pro-Invoice #32620261 dated March 26, 2026, issued by Manufacturer, is hereby incorporated by reference as the governing price document for per-unit pricing under this Amendment. The per-SKU quantities listed in the Pro-Invoice (7,500 per SKU) represent a target allocation; the actual allocation across SKUs is flexible per Section 1.2, provided the aggregate volume commitment of 22,500 units is met.

Article 3

Ingredients and Formulation

3.1

Company-Supplied Ingredients. Notwithstanding Section 2.3 of the Original Agreement, Company shall source, pay for, and supply the following ingredients directly to Manufacturer for all production runs under this Amendment:

  • Fragrance: OnScent Pacific Air NF (or equivalent approved natural fragrance)
  • SO-Resisthyal-25 (hyaluronic acid complex)
  • SymHair Force 1631 (hair strengthening peptide)

Company shall deliver the above ingredients to Manufacturer in sufficient quantities prior to the start of each production run. The costs of Company-supplied ingredients are borne entirely by Company and are not reflected in the per-unit pricing set forth in Article 2.

3.2

Required Active Ingredients. All formulations produced under this Amendment shall include the following active and functional ingredients at the specified concentrations:

Ingredient Concentration Supplied By
SO-Resisthyal-25 1% of batch volume Company
SymHair Force 1631 1% of batch volume Company
OnScent Pacific Air NF (fragrance) 1% of batch volume Company
Camellia Sinensis (green tea) leaf extract Per formulation specification Manufacturer
Curcuma Longa (turmeric) root extract Per formulation specification Manufacturer
Beta Vulgaris (beetroot) root extract Per formulation specification Manufacturer

Concentrations listed as "1% of batch volume" are fixed unless otherwise agreed in writing by both parties. Concentrations listed as "per formulation specification" shall be as defined in the product specification sheet provided by Company per Article 8.

3.3

Formulation Integrity. Manufacturer shall not omit, substitute, or alter the concentration of any required active ingredient listed in Section 3.2 without prior written approval from Company. Any batch found to be missing a required ingredient or outside the specified concentration shall be considered non-conforming per Article 8.4.

3.4

Company-Supplied Packaging Materials. Notwithstanding Section 2.3 of the Original Agreement, Company shall source, pay for, and supply all packaging materials to Manufacturer, including but not limited to bottles, pumps, labels, and shipper boxes. The costs of Company-supplied packaging materials are borne entirely by Company and are not reflected in the per-unit pricing set forth in Article 2.

3.5

Manufacturer's Scope of Work. Manufacturer shall be responsible for bulk formulation, filling, coding, labeling, packing in shipper boxes, palletizing, and stability testing. Manufacturer remains responsible for procuring all other materials and ingredients per Section 2.3 of the Original Agreement, including but not limited to base surfactants, conditioning agents, preservatives, and solubilizers.

3.6

Third-Party Testing. Third-party laboratory micro testing shall be at Company's expense. Company may designate the testing laboratory and testing scope for each production run.

3.7

Manufacturing Location and Delivery. All production under this Amendment shall be performed at Teamwork Packaging, San Bernardino, California. All prices are ex-factory at said location. Transportation from the factory is at Company's expense and arrangement.

3.8

Storage and Accountability. Manufacturer shall properly store and maintain all Company-supplied ingredients and packaging materials per the specifications in Sections 1.2 and 3.2 of the Original Agreement, and shall account for all Company-supplied materials, reporting quantities used and any remaining inventory after each production run.

Payment Terms for Remaining Balance. After application of the credit, the remaining balance for each production run shall be paid as follows:

  • 50% due upon submission of Purchase Order (before production commences)
  • 50% due upon delivery and acceptance of finished goods per Section 4.5 of the Original Agreement (Company shall have 5 business days to inspect and accept or reject)
4.6

Lab Fee Credit. For clarity, the $3,500.00 lab fee credit under Section 5.3 of the Original Agreement remains available for future product development and formulation work, and is not applied to production costs under this Amendment.

Article 5

Outstanding Obligations

5.1

Manufacturer acknowledges that 400 units of AWARE 9.1 Professional Shampoo 300ML from Production Run 1 remain undelivered (2,500 ordered, 2,100 received). These units were included in prior payments.

5.2

The 400 outstanding shampoo units shall be manufactured and delivered alongside the next production run under this Amendment, at no additional charge to Company.

5.3

Manufacturer may fulfill the 400 units in retail-sized (300ML) or bulk gallon format, at Company's election.

Article 6

Production Schedule

6.1

Initial Order. The first production order under this Amendment has been placed per Invoice #32620261 dated March 26, 2026, comprising 3,000 units of Shampoo 300ML and 3,000 units of Conditioner 300ML (6,000 units total).

6.2

Subsequent Orders. Remaining units shall be ordered via Purchase Orders per Article 7. Part shipments are permitted with mutual pre-approval of quantity and timing by both parties.

6.3

Minimum Production Guarantee. Company shall order a minimum of 15,000 units (total across all SKUs) on or before September 26, 2027, at the prices set forth in Article 2.1. The remaining units, up to a total of 22,500, may be ordered after September 26, 2027 subject to price renegotiation per Article 2.4.

Note: The 400 shampoo units owed per Article 5 shall be included at no additional charge when a shampoo Purchase Order is placed.

6.4

Manufacturer shall provide a minimum of 30 days notice if any production milestone is at risk of delay.

6.5

Lead time for each production run shall not exceed 45 days from receipt of deposit and Purchase Order.

6.6

Late Delivery. The late delivery penalties set forth in Section 4.6 of the Original Agreement (1% of invoice value per day of delay, up to 50% of total invoice value) shall apply to deliveries under this Amendment.

Article 7

Purchase Orders

7.1

Company shall submit a written Purchase Order for each production run, specifying:

  • SKUs and quantities
  • Per-unit pricing (per Article 2)
  • Requested delivery date
  • Delivery location
  • Payment schedule
7.2

Manufacturer shall confirm acceptance of each Purchase Order within 7 business days.

7.3

No production shall commence without a signed Purchase Order.

Article 8

Quality and Specifications

8.1

All quality standards, specifications, manufacturing processes, and compliance requirements set forth in Sections 1.2, 2.1 through 2.9, and 3.1 through 3.11 of the Original Agreement remain in full force and effect.

8.2

Specification Sheet. Company shall provide a product specification sheet for each SKU prior to each production run. The specification sheet shall define the required parameters including, but not limited to, pH range, viscosity, color, scent profile, active ingredient concentrations, and any other quality criteria. Manufacturer's quality control process shall verify that each batch conforms to the Company-provided specification sheet before filling and packaging.

8.3

Manufacturer shall provide Certificates of Analysis (COA) and batch records for each production run, demonstrating conformance to the specification sheet.

8.4

Non-Conforming Product. Any batch that does not meet the specifications defined in the Company-provided specification sheet, including but not limited to failure of microbiological challenge testing, failure of stability testing, deviation from pH, viscosity, color, scent, or active ingredient concentration parameters, or failure of any other standardized quality or safety testing, shall be deemed non-conforming ("Non-Conforming Product").

8.5

Manufacturer's Remediation Obligations. Upon identification of Non-Conforming Product, Manufacturer shall, at Manufacturer's sole cost and expense:

  • Reformulate and reproduce the entire affected batch to meet specifications, including all material, labor, and production costs;
  • Bear the cost of any additional testing (including third-party laboratory testing) required to verify that the replacement batch meets specifications;
  • Deliver the replacement batch within the same lead time as the original order (45 days per Section 6.5), starting from the date the non-conformance is confirmed.
8.6

Consequential Costs and Liabilities. Manufacturer shall be liable for all direct costs and losses incurred by Company as a result of Non-Conforming Product, including but not limited to:

  • Cost of Company-supplied ingredients and packaging materials consumed in the non-conforming batch;
  • Disposal costs for non-conforming finished goods or raw materials rendered unusable;
  • Expedited shipping or production costs necessary to meet Company's delivery commitments to its customers;
  • Any penalties, chargebacks, or damages assessed against Company by its retail, wholesale, or distribution partners as a direct result of the non-conforming product or resulting delivery delays.
8.7

No Limitation on Existing Remedies. The remedies set forth in Sections 8.5 and 8.6 are in addition to, and do not limit, any other remedies available to Company under the Original Agreement, this Amendment, or applicable law, including but not limited to the late delivery penalties in Section 6.6 if the replacement batch causes a delivery delay.

Article 9

Term and Renewal

9.1

The parties acknowledge that the initial one-year term of the Original Agreement expired on or about July 22, 2025, and that both parties have continued to operate under its terms in good faith since that date.

9.2

The Original Agreement is hereby renewed and extended through the earlier of (a) completion and delivery of all 22,500 committed units and fulfillment of all outstanding obligations, or (b) September 26, 2028. This extension accounts for the fact that up to 7,500 units may be ordered after the September 26, 2027 price validity date per Article 1.3.

9.3

Further renewals beyond the term set forth in Section 9.2 shall be by mutual written agreement per Section 6.1 of the Original Agreement.

Article 10

General Provisions

10.1

Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms and conditions of the Original Agreement and the October 2024 Addendum remain in full force and effect.

10.2

In the event of any conflict between this Amendment and the Original Agreement, this Amendment shall control.

10.3

This Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.

10.4

This Amendment constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements relating thereto.

10.5

Governing Law and Disputes. This Amendment shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California per Section 13.1 of the Original Agreement. Any disputes arising out of this Amendment shall be resolved per Section 13.2 of the Original Agreement (California state and federal courts).
