What AI "Sees" When Recommending Salons

AI assistants synthesize information from multiple sources to make recommendations. Here's what they look for:

Google Business Profile Completeness

Complete profiles with services, photos, and regular posts rank higher.

Review Volume AND Recency

Recent reviews signal you're active and in-demand. Stale reviews hurt rankings.

Specialty Services Mentioned

Salons with clear specialties are recommended for those specific queries.

Consistency Across Platforms

Same name, address, and phone everywhere builds AI trust.

💡 The Specialty Advantage

Salons with clearly defined specialties (like scalp treatments) are 2.3x more likely to be recommended for those specific queries. Generalist positioning means competing with everyone.
