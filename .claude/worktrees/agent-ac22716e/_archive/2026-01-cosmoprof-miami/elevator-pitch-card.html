Pitch Reference Card
Keep in pocket at the show. Memorize the 30-second version, adapt the longer ones based on audience.
30 Seconds
Walking the floor, quick introductions, passing by a booth
We're AWARE Hair - the only professional-exclusive, clean-formulation scalp care brand. Our founder is a trichologist with 40 years in the chair. We have a 96% salon repurchase rate and zero returns. Scalp care is a $14.7 billion market growing at 7.1% CAGR, and we own the white space.
Pivot to: "What channel are you in?" or "Are your salons asking about scalp services?"
60 Seconds
Engaged interest at your booth, waiting in line together, cocktail hour
AWARE is professional scalp care - founded by a master trichologist who spent 40 years treating scalp issues that no product on the market could solve. Our 9.1 series uses patent-pending actives from Givaudan and Symrise - clinical-grade ingredients in a completely clean formula. No sulfates, no silicones, no parabens.

We're salon-exclusive - we never compete with our partners through DTC. The results: 96% repurchase rate, zero product returns, and salons ordering every 28-35 days. The scalp care market is $14.7 billion, growing at 7.1% CAGR. We're looking for the right distribution partner to help us scale from 35 salons to 200+.
Pivot to: "Can I show you the sell-through data?" or "We have a 90-day pilot with a full credit guarantee."
2 Minutes
Scheduled meeting, seated conversation, genuine interest expressed
Let me give you the full picture. Tina Mui, our CEO, is a master trichologist who founded over a dozen salons across Southern California. For 40 years she watched clients come in with scalp issues - hard water damage, pollution exposure, stress-related conditions - and every product she tried was either too harsh or ineffective.

So she built AWARE. Our 9.1 Professional Series uses patent-pending actives: ResistHyal from Givaudan delivers 189x more hydration than placebo; SymHair Force from Symrise reduces breakage 82%. All in a formula that's sulfate-free, silicone-free, paraben-free, vegan, and made in our FDA-registered LA facility.

The results speak for themselves. 35+ salon partners. 96% repurchase rate versus an industry average of 60-70%. Average reorder cycle of 28-35 days versus the typical 45-60. Zero returns. Annual value per door is $6-12K.

We're at an inflection point. The scalp care market is $14.7 billion globally, growing at 7.1% CAGR, and there's no dominant professional-exclusive clean brand in the space. We're looking for a distribution partner who can help us go from 35 doors to 200+ while protecting the professional channel.
Pivot to: "I'd love to walk you through our pilot proposal - 90 days, territory exclusive, full credit guarantee if it doesn't perform."

Tailor by Audience
Distributors
Lead with: recurring revenue, zero returns
"Your reps sell it once, then collect recurring commissions. 96% repurchase means near-zero churn."
Retailers
Lead with: consumer demand, small footprint
"18M organic views, active search demand, $500-750 revenue per sq ft per month. 3-product line, simple merchandising."
Salon Owners
Lead with: scalp services revenue, client retention
"Add $70-250 scalp services to your menu. Clients rebook for treatment, take home the retail. Recurring revenue."
Media / Press
Lead with: founder story, trend angle
"Trichologist-founded after 40 years. The 'skinification of scalp' meets professional heritage. Used on Emmy-winning productions."
Avoid These
  • - Don't trash competitors by name (position as "white space" instead)
  • - Don't lead with price or discounts (lead with repurchase and results)
  • - Don't say "we're small" (say "we're selective about partners")
  • - Don't oversell DTC potential (we're professional-exclusive, that's the value prop)
  • - Don't promise timeline you can't deliver (be honest about capacity)
