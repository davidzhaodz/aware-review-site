5. Shelf Life & Storage Requirements

Product Dating

  • Shelf Life: 24 months from date of manufacture
  • Date Coding: Lot number includes manufacture date
  • Expiration: Printed on bottle bottom and outer carton

Storage Conditions

  • Temperature: Room temperature (59-77°F / 15-25°C)
  • Environment: Store away from direct sunlight and excessive heat
  • Humidity: Avoid high humidity storage areas
  • Orientation: Store upright to prevent leakage

First In, First Out (FIFO)

Product is date-coded to support proper inventory rotation practices.
