07

Objection Handling

"We already have scalp care brands."

"That's great - it shows the category is working for you. Here's the difference: we're salon-exclusive with clinical-grade, clean formulation. Most scalp care is either too harsh (clarifying shampoos) or doesn't actually work. We fill the gap with real efficacy backed by trichology science. And our 96% repurchase rate means less SKU churn for your team - salons don't try us once and move on."

"You're a West Coast brand - why would PA salons care?"

"Fair question. Two things: First, environmental scalp issues are universal - hard water, pollution, seasonal changes. Pittsburgh has plenty of that. Second, you'd be first - we have no presence in PA/WV yet. That's a competitive advantage for your salons before other distributors pick us up."

"You're too small / too new."

"We're early - that's exactly right. But that's why we're having this conversation now. You get in on a brand with proven product-market fit before it scales. Most brands come to distributors after they've built DTC that competes with your salons. We're doing it the right way - distribution first."

"What kind of marketing support do you offer?"

"Three things: Stylist training - virtual and in-person. Our CEO Tina is a 40-year master trichologist who speaks their language. Point-of-sale materials your reps can use. And social content that can be co-branded - we've generated 18 million organic views with content stylists actually want to share."

"What are your margins?"

"Premium positioning - 40% retail margin for salons through distribution, 50% direct. Core range is $55-60 retail. On the distributor side, we're flexible on structure - happy to walk through the numbers in detail once we get you samples and you see the product quality."

"Send me some information."

"Absolutely - I'll email you our one-pager right after this call. But honestly, the product sells itself - can I send you samples too? Best way to evaluate is to try it. Your stylists will understand immediately why the repurchase rate is so high."
