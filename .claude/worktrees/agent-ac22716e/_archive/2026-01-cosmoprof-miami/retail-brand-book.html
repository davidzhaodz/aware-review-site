Our Origin
The AWARE Story

After 40 years behind the chair and founding over a dozen salons across Southern California, master trichologist Tina Mui saw the same problem every day: clients with scalp issues that no product solved.

Hard water. Pollution. UV damage. Stress. The modern environment attacks the scalp in ways traditional haircare was never designed to address.

AWARE was built because Tina needed it for her own clients. No brand was doing scalp care right: clinical actives without harsh chemicals, professional-grade results without professional-only complexity.

"I built AWARE because after four decades, I still couldn't find a product I fully trusted for my clients' scalps."
Tina Mui, Founder and Master Trichologist
