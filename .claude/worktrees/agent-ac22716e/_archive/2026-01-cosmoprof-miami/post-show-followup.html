Day-of-Show Tasks (Jan 29, evening)
  • Sort all lead capture sheets by priority (H/W/C)
  • Enter leads into CRM/spreadsheet with notes from conversations
  • Photo/scan all business cards, attach to lead records
  • Draft personalized notes for each Hot lead (reference specific conversation points)
  • Confirm sample kit shipping list (who gets samples shipped?)
  • Team debrief: top 5 leads, any commitments made, next steps agreed
Key Principle
  • - Speed wins. Hot leads should hear from you within 24 hours of show close.
  • - Personalize every email. Reference something specific from your conversation.
  • - Always include a clear next step (call, sample, proposal, meeting).
  • - Use the show special deadline (Feb 5) as natural urgency without being pushy.

Post-Show Master Checklist
  • Sort and classify all leads (Hot / Warm / Cool) - Day 0
  • Enter all leads into CRM with full notes - Day 0
  • Send Hot lead emails (personalized) - Day 1
  • Send Warm lead emails + confirm sample shipments - Day 1
  • Send Cool lead emails - Day 3
  • Follow up with Hot leads (call if no reply) - Day 3
  • Ship all sample kits promised at show - Day 1-3
  • Send sell-through data to interested distributors - Day 3
  • Hot lead final push (deadline reminder) - Day 7
  • Warm lead check-in (samples arrived?) - Day 7
  • Warm lead final touch / nurture offer - Day 14
  • Add non-responders to monthly newsletter - Day 14
  • Process any orders received during follow-up window
  • Update CRM with final status for each lead
  • Team retrospective: what worked, what to improve for next show
