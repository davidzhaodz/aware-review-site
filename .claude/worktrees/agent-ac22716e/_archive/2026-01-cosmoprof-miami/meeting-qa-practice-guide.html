📚 Brand Story & Vision 6 questions
Tell me about AWARE Hair. What's the story?Common

AWARE Hair was founded by Tina Mui, a master trichologist with 40 years of experience. She founded 12 salons in her career and kept seeing the same unsolved problems: clients with scalp damage from hard water, pollution, and UV exposure.

Existing scalp care products were either medical-focused on thinning hair or just marketed shampoos with no real technology. So she developed AWARE - a patent-pending formulation using dual-weight hyaluronic acid and Tahitian microalgae specifically for environmental scalp health.

It's clinical without being clinical-looking. Premium, clean, and it actually works.

What does "environmental scalp health" mean?Common

Environmental scalp health addresses the everyday damage most people don't realize they're experiencing:

  • Hard water damage - mineral buildup that dries and irritates the scalp
  • UV stress - sun damage that affects scalp health just like skin
  • Pollution - particulates that clog follicles and cause inflammation
  • Climate changes - humidity shifts, air conditioning, heating

We're not targeting thinning hair or dandruff - those are medical conditions. We're addressing the scalp stress that affects everyone, especially people who travel, live in cities, or have hard water at home.

Who is your target customer?Common

Our B2B customer is the health-conscious, quality-focused salon - typically upscale full-service salons with clients aged 28-55 who care about ingredients and wellness.

The end consumer is someone who:

  • Invests in quality skincare and wants the same for their scalp
  • Experiences scalp dryness, sensitivity, or irritation
  • Lives in a city, travels frequently, or has hard water
  • Prefers clean, professional-grade products
What makes AWARE different from other scalp care brands?Common

Three key differentiators:

  • Salon-exclusive distribution - We never undercut our professional partners with aggressive DTC. This builds trust and loyalty.
  • Patent-pending formulation - Our dual-weight hyaluronic acid technology isn't just marketing. It's clinically shown to deliver 189x more hydration than placebo.
  • Environmental positioning - We own the "environmental scalp health" space. Competitors focus on thinning hair (medical) or general scalp care (generic). We have a specific, underserved niche.

Plus, our 96% B2B repurchase rate proves the product works. You don't get that with marketing - you get that with results.

  • Tina Mui, CEO - Master trichologist, 40 years experience, founded 12 salons. She's the product visionary and brings deep professional credibility.

What's your vision for AWARE in 5 years?Moderate

We want to be the definitive professional scalp care brand - what Olaplex did for bond repair, we want to do for scalp health.

That means:

  • National distribution through major professional channels
  • Expanding from 3 products to a comprehensive scalp wellness line

📦 Distribution & Pricing 8 questions

Professional-exclusive - we only sell through salons, never mass retail.

Currently direct-to-salon in the US. We're at Cosmoprof specifically to find the right distribution partners to scale while maintaining our salon-exclusive positioning.

What are your distributor margins?Common

Tiered structure based on volume:

  • National (500+ doors): 50% off retail
  • Regional (100-499 doors): 45% off retail
  • Local (50-99 doors): 40% off retail

Salons retail at $55-60, so even at 40% discount they're making $22-24 per unit. Premium pricing means everyone makes money.

What's your opening order requirement?Common

Depends on distributor size:

  • Regional: $10,000 opening, $2,500 reorder
  • Local: $5,000 opening, $1,500 reorder

We're flexible for the right partners. The goal is ensuring you have proper inventory to actually sell against - not just taking orders to hit numbers.

What are your payment terms?Common

Standard professional terms:

  • Credit application required for Net 30 terms
