1. DTC & Channel Conflict

Q: What about your DTC channel? Won't you compete with our salon customers?

A: We restrict DTC in partner territories once we reach 12+ active salon accounts through your distribution (roughly $75K annual run rate). During ramp-up, we maintain full retail pricing on DTC with zero discounting - we never compete with salons on price. Geographic restrictions can be implemented on our website within 30 days of partnership signing.

Why 12 accounts? That threshold replaces our direct consumer revenue in that territory, making the transition financially viable for both parties.

Q: How do we know you won't undercut us online?

A: Three binding policies:

  • Strict MAP enforcement - $55-60 retail pricing is non-negotiable, everywhere
  • Territory restrictions - We geo-block DTC sales in partner territories after ramp-up
  • No promotional undercutting - Salons always have pricing advantage through wholesale terms

Your salon customers buy at 40-50% off retail ($27.50-33.00 wholesale) and sell at $55-60. Our DTC (when active) never discounts below MAP. The salon margin is protected.

Q: Can you shut off DTC completely in our territory from Day 1?

A: Yes, if the partnership includes a minimum first-year purchase commitment of $75K in the territory. That ensures both parties have real skin in the game. Without that commitment, we use a 90-day ramp schedule: DTC restrictions start Day 1 (no paid ads, no local targeting), full shutdown within 90 days once your orders are flowing.
