Model Assumptions

  • Reorder Point = (Avg Daily Sales x Lead Time) + Safety Stock
  • Safety Stock = (Max Daily Sales x Max Lead Time) - (Avg Daily Sales x Avg Lead Time)
  • Max Daily Sales = Avg Daily Sales x 1.5 (accounts for demand spikes)
  • Reorder quantity targets 45 days of supply plus safety stock buffer
  • COGS figures are estimates - replace with actuals from manufacturer invoices
  • Salon velocity defaults assume even distribution across accounts
