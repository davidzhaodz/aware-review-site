The Science Behind AWARE

AWARE Hair was founded on a clinical observation, not a marketing brief. After more than four decades working with clients in a professional salon setting, co-founder Tina Mui - a licensed cosmetologist and master trichologist - identified a consistent gap in professional haircare: no product line was designed to support the scalp environment as its primary function. AWARE was built to fill that gap.

Why the Scalp Environment Matters

The scalp is not simply the surface from which hair grows. It is a living microenvironment with its own microbiome, sebum balance, pH, and moisture barrier. When that environment is disrupted - by harsh surfactants, synthetic additives, or the cumulative stress of chemical treatments - the conditions for healthy hair growth become harder to maintain. Most professional haircare addresses the strand after the fact. AWARE addresses the scalp environment before disruption takes hold.

This is the distinction between reactive and proactive scalp care. Reactive products intervene when something has already gone wrong. Proactive care supports the scalp environment continuously, through daily use, to help maintain the conditions that favor healthy hair over time.

The AWAREBIO Complex

The AWAREBIO Complex is AWARE's patent-pending proprietary formulation. Every ingredient was selected for a documented role in scalp and hair health, not for fragrance or texture alone.

  • Biofermented Tahitian Microalgae (SymHair Force 1631) - Supports the anagen (active growth) phase of the hair cycle. Formulated with ingredients clinically shown to support a 10.5% increase in growth-phase hair and 82% improvement in breakage resistance in system testing (Symrise, 30-volunteer clinical trial, 3 months).
  • Dual-Weight Hyaluronic Acid (ResistHyal) - Two molecular weights penetrate at different depths to deliver sustained scalp hydration. Formulated with ingredients shown to support up to 61% softness improvement and 48% volume improvement after 28 days, with a 65% reduction in brittle hair under mechanical and thermal stress (Givaudan clinical data).
  • Vegan Keratin Amino Acids - Reinforce the hair shaft's protein structure from the outside in, reducing breakage and supporting the integrity of color-treated and chemically processed hair.
  • Organic Green Tea Extract (EGCG) - One of nature's most studied polyphenol antioxidants, recognized in dermatological literature for its role in neutralizing oxidative stress at the follicle level and supporting dermal papilla cell health.
  • Organic Turmeric Extract - Curcumin, the active compound in turmeric, is associated in clinical literature with calming scalp inflammation and supporting a balanced scalp environment.
  • Organic Beet Root Extract - Rich in betaine, a natural humectant that helps the scalp retain moisture and resist osmotic stress from hard water and environmental dryness.

Formulation Philosophy

We eliminated sulfates, parabens, silicones, phthalates, synthetic fragrances, and synthetic dyes from every formula - not because these ingredients are universally harmful, but because they introduce disruption to the scalp environment that our actives are designed to support. Choosing botanical actives over synthetic shortcuts is a harder formulation path. It is also a more consistent one.

Every product in the 9·1 Professional collection is crafted in small batches in a GMP-certified California laboratory, formulated to EU cosmetic standards, and tested to be color-safe, pH-balanced, and suitable for daily use.

Trichology at the Foundation

Tina Mui's credentials as both a licensed cosmetologist and a master trichologist are not incidental to AWARE's formulation approach - they are the reason the AWAREBIO Complex exists in the form it does. Trichology is the study of the scalp and hair, distinct from general cosmetology in its clinical depth. Every formula begins with a question Tina has asked at the chair since the beginning: what does the scalp actually need, and what is getting in the way?
