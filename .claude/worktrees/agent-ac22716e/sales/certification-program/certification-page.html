What Your Team Will Learn
Scalp Environment Science How UV, water quality, pollution, and climate affect follicle health - and why it differs by location.
The 4R Protocol Reset, Repair, Reinforce, Reactivate. A systematic framework for assessing and treating scalp concerns.
Common Scalp Conditions Visual identification of dryness, buildup, sensitivity, oiliness, and thinning. When to treat vs. when to refer.
Client Consultation Technique The AWARE framework: Ask, Where, Assess, Report, Educate. Turn every appointment into a scalp health conversation.
Service Integration How to position scalp treatments as $35-55 add-ons. Pricing strategies, scripts for handling objections, and upsell techniques.
Product Knowledge Formulation science behind each AWARE product. How to match products to individual scalp profiles for personalized recommendations.
Continuing Education and Recertification
Scalp science evolves, and so does your expertise. Certified specialists receive ongoing education updates, access to advanced training modules as they launch, and priority invitations to AWARE workshops and industry events. Recertification is a lightweight refresh, not a full course retake, keeping your team current without disrupting your schedule.
