Who can I refer?

Any licensed salon, spa, or independent stylist who isn't yet an AWARE partner.

When do I receive my credit?

Within 7 days of your referral's first wholesale order. We'll email you when it's applied.

Is there a limit?

No cap. Refer as many salons as you like. Tier rewards accumulate over the calendar year.
