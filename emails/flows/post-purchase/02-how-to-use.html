Scalp Care Ritual

A gentle guide to caring for
scalp, strand, and self.

Prepare

Begin with Intention

Set the tone. Let the warmth of the water open your hair and your senses. You are entering a space of care - for your scalp, your strands, and your self. This is your time.

Step 1

Reset and Purify the Scalp

Gently massage onto the scalp and invite stillness. Allow 1-3 minutes for the bioactive ingredients to penetrate and nourish the scalp. Add water and let the lather bloom. If needed, repeat.

Step 2

Repair with Care

Apply a generous amount of conditioner, working your way from ends to roots, massaging remaining product onto the scalp. Let it absorb for 3-5 minutes as you gently detangle. Use as a scalp mask and leave it on for 10-15 minutes for extra nourishment.

Step 3

Reinforce Against Damage

Apply to damp hair to add 360 degree protection, light hold, and all-day shine. Wait 1-3 minutes for it to absorb. Comb through and style as you wish - with ease, confidence, and grace.

Step 4

Reactivate with Gua Sha

Gently massage the scalp for 3-5 minutes using a 100% sandalwood scalp gua sha, or your hands, to promote microcirculation, tension release, and relaxation. Allow yourself to savor the moment.
