◆ The 4R Protocol ◆

Step 1: Reset

Tonight, wash with AWARE Shampoo

Massage into scalp for 60 seconds. Focus on your scalp, not your hair - this is where the work happens.

Step 2: Repair

Follow with AWARE Conditioner

Leave for 3-5 minutes. Apply from mid-length to ends, then work into scalp. The repair happens during the wait.

Step 3: Reinforce

Apply Leave-In to towel-dried hair

Right after showering, apply to towel-dried hair. Use 1-2 pumps for short hair, 3-4 for long. This protects against environmental damage all day.

Step 4: Reactivate

Stimulate your scalp 3-5x per week

Use the AWARE Sandalwood Gua Sha with light-medium pressure to boost circulation and release tension. Don't have one? Use the pads of your fingers - gentle, circular motions across your entire scalp for 2-3 minutes.
