Head Spa Service Protocol

Step-by-step guide for delivering a professional head spa treatment

Service Flow (45-60 minutes)

Consultation + Assessment 5 minutes
Scalp assessment, client goals, water quality history, contraindication check
Deep Cleanse 10 minutes
Double cleanse: first wash lifts surface debris, second normalizes scalp pH. Focus on follicle clarity.
9-1 Daily Scalp Cleanser
Treatment Mask 10 minutes
Apply scalp mask from roots through ends. Tailor to client need: hydration, barrier repair, or detox.
9-1 Hydrating Scalp Mask
Scalp Massage + Gua Sha 15 minutes
Extended massage for circulation, tension release, and product absorption. Gua sha along meridian lines.
Sandalwood Gua Sha Comb
Steam (Optional) 5 minutes
Warm steam to open follicles and enhance mask absorption. Skip if client has active inflammation.
Rinse + Finish 10 minutes
Cool rinse to close cuticle. Apply leave-in for UV defense. Style as desired.
9-1 Professional Leave-In
