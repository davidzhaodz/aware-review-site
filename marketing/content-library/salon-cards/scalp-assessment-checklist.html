2-Minute Scalp Assessment

A quick consultation framework for every client visit

Visual Inspection Zones (1 min)

Part the hair in 4-5 zones and look for inconsistencies:

CrownCheck for thinning, oil distribution, flaking patterns
TemplesLook for recession, sensitivity, redness
NapeAssess buildup, follicle clarity, moisture level
Part LineEvaluate width, skin visibility, color consistency

Visual Assessment Checklist

  • Redness or inflammation in any zone
  • Flaking (note: oily/yellow vs dry/white)
  • Visible buildup around follicles
  • Uneven oil distribution
  • Thinning or widening at part line
  • Signs of scratching or irritation

Client History Questions (1 min)

  • What is your water quality at home? (City water, well, filtered?)
  • What products are you currently using on your scalp?
  • Any recent changes to medications, diet, or stress levels?
  • How often are you washing, and has that changed recently?
  • Any persistent itching, tightness, or sensitivity?
Decision Tree
Proceed to Service Healthy scalp, no mineral concerns, no active conditions
Prep First Buildup visible, hard water area, pH irregularity, flaking
Treat + Reschedule Open sores, active psoriasis flare, chemical burns
