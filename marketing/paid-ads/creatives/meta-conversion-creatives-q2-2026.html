Strategy

What We Learned, How We Apply It

KilgourMD runs ~1,700 ads and buys every customer. We have 50+ salons and a 96% reorder rate. Their playbook tells us what converts in scalp care - we just need to run it through our channel.

Long-Form Education

Scalp care requires education before purchase. KilgourMD runs 1,000-4,000 word ad copy. We adapt to 300-800 words - same educational depth, calibrated for AWARE's elevated restraint.

Named-Enemy Positioning

KilgourMD names minoxidil as the enemy. AWARE names sulfates, silicones, and "one-size-fits-all" drugstore products. Same challenger energy, different battlefield.

Funnel to Owned Asset

KilgourMD funnels to a marketing quiz. AWARE funnels to the Scalp Environment Report - real environmental data, not segmentation theater. Same conversion mechanic, genuine utility.

The Sulfate Takedown

AWARE-CONV-02-SulfateTakedown-Education
Traffic / Conversion Segment C + Interest: Clean Beauty Scalp Report
Primary Text

The shampoo aisle is lying to you. Not maliciously - just lazily.

Sodium lauryl sulfate strips the scalp's acid mantle in a single wash. That barrier took your body 14 days to build. The foaming agent in your $8 shampoo removes it in 90 seconds. Then your scalp overcompensates - producing excess sebum, shifting pH, creating the exact conditions that cause the flaking, itching, and oiliness you bought the shampoo to fix.

This is not a marketing claim. It is documented in dermatological literature. And it is the reason "sulfate-free" alone is not enough.

The real question is what replaces the sulfate. Most brands swap in milder surfactants and call it clean. That is a start. But it ignores the second problem: what is happening to your scalp between washes.

Environmental factors - hard water minerals, UV radiation, airborne pollutants, humidity shifts - are degrading your scalp barrier every day. No shampoo addresses this because no shampoo was designed to.

The AWARE 9-1 Professional Shampoo was. It uses our patent-pending AWAREBIO Complex to actively support barrier repair, microbiome balance, and environmental defense. Nine clinically-informed benefits in a single, salon-grade formula.

We are not the drugstore aisle. We are what your stylist actually recommends.
