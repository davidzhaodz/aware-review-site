Algorithm Tactics - Quick Reference
★ Saves = 5x Reach
Saves carry 5x more algorithmic weight than likes. Design content to be saved: checklists, frameworks, reference material.
↗ Links Kill Reach
Links in the post body reduce distribution ~60%. Always put URLs in the first comment instead.
⏲ First 30 Min
The first 30 minutes are critical for algorithmic distribution. Time posts when your audience is active.
# 1-3 Hashtags Max
Mix one broad (#beauty) with one niche (#scalphealth). More than 3 dilutes distribution.
💬 Commenting > Posting
15 min of substantive commenting daily is the single highest-ROI LinkedIn activity. Comment before you post.
✎ Specifics Win
Posts with concrete specifics (names, metrics, timeframes) get 3-4x reach vs. generic content.

Your ZIP Code Knows More About Your Hair
Scalp Health Formulation
We built a tool that pulls real environmental data for your ZIP code: - UV index from weather stations - Air quality from EPA monitors - Water hardness in parts per million from EPA databases - Humidity, temperature, ozone levels Then it calculates a personalized scalp health risk score. Hard water above 180 PPM? Your scalp's moisture barrier is compromised. UV index above 7 with low humidity? Oxidative stress on the scalp. High ozone + high particulate matter? Inflammation trigger. This isn't a quiz. It's real government data applied to scalp science. Most beauty brands ask you 5 generic questions and recommend the same product. We look at what's actually hitting your scalp every day. The result: a personalized report that tells you something no product quiz can. The scalp care conversation is moving from "what's your hair type?" to "what's your environment?" What's one industry where personalization is still mostly fake?
