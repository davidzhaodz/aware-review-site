Print Production Notes

Paper Stock

18-20pt cotton or textured uncoated. Soft-touch laminate both sides for velvety feel.

Cream base stock (not bright white)

Gold Foil Elements

Hot foil stamp on: AWARE monogram, wordmark, corner ornaments, border lines. Matte gold finish.

PMS 7407 C or closest matte gold

Botanical Art

Lemon illustrations printed in spot color at low opacity. Could also be blind debossed for tactile effect.

Edge Treatment

Gold edge painting on all four sides. Visible when card sits in gift packaging.
