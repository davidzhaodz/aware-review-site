AWARE HAIR08
Three Phases

The hair growth cycle.

Anagen
2–6 YRS · Growth
the long, active phase — follicle produces the hair shaft
Catagen
1–2 WK · Transition
follicle retracts
Telogen
3–4 MO · Shed & Renew
old hair releases, new hair begins
◆ 85% of follicles are in anagen at any given moment

Technique

Move the tissue,

3–5 min · 2–3× per week
