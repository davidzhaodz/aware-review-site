60-75 min/week total ▼
Monday - 30 min

Open with: "Is what we're doing this week acquiring salons or executing partnerships?" If the answer is neither, stop that work.

Review: What moved? What's stuck? Any inbound that changes priorities?

Each person confirms their top 3 for the week.

Execution Score (David)
Score: What % of planned tasks got done?

Below 70% for 2 weeks = too many initiatives, something must be killed.

Move completed items, pull next from backlog.

ICE-score any new opportunities. Update kill list.
