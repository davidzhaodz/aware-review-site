Workflow A: HeyGen Avatar
Primary
Budget: $29/mo Creator plan. Avatar IV = 5 min/mo (premium), Standard = unlimited.
Step A1: Reference Footage (One-Time)
  • 2-5 min continuous footage, 1080p+, natural lighting
  • Head & shoulders, centered, eye contact with lens
  • Neutral solid-color top, simple background
  • Separate consent video per HeyGen's script
  • Export MP4, H.264 codec

Workflow B: Kling AI B-Roll
Supplementary
Budget: $10/mo Standard plan. 660 credits/mo. Queue times: 30min-24hr
Step B1: B-Roll Generation (Primary)
Write specific visual prompts. Submit to Kling 2-3 days before needed. Never rely on same-day output.
Example prompts: "Close-up of a woman's scalp being examined under salon lighting, warm tones, 5s" / "Product application on scalp, professional hands, salon environment"
Step B2: Avatar 2.0 (Backup)
Still photo of Tina + audio file = talking head clip. Quality is OK for social stories, not hero Reels. Supplement to HeyGen, not replacement.
Step B3: Director Memory (Consistency)
Kling's unique feature: maintains character and scene consistency across generations. Generate a reference clip first, enable Director Memory, all subsequent clips match the aesthetic. Use for series content (e.g., all 4R Protocol clips).
