Design System Applied (Both Directions)
6-Element Visual Glue (consistent across every slide):
1. Slide number - top right, JetBrains Mono, "03 / 07" format
2. Gold accent line - bottom edge, gradient fade
3. Font system - Orpheus Pro (headlines) + DM Sans (body) + JetBrains Mono (labels/numbers)
4. Color palette - Fixed: dark teal OR cream background, gold accent, sage for secondary
5. Section kicker - top left, monospace, uppercase, spaced tracking
6. Edge margins - 15% on all sides (162px at 1080px = 54px at 360px scale)

What changed from current carousel:
- Removed ALL photo backgrounds (solid color only)
- Removed burgundy, teal-cyan, random colors (locked to brand palette)
- Org chart: grid layout fills space, text at readable sizes
- Morning slide: same background as all other slides (no more cream outlier)
- Every slide shares the same structural skeleton

Research specs applied:
- Headline: ~80-100px at 1080px (26-33px at 360px scale)
- Body: ~33-36px at 1080px (11-12px at 360px scale)
- Words per slide: ~20-35 (well under 50 max)
- Safe zones: content stays 15% from all edges
