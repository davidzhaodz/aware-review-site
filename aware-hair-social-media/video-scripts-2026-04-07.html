Production Notes

Camera & Lighting

  • Medium close-up, eye level
  • Natural, warm lighting
  • Professional but not overproduced
  • Position teleprompter near lens
  • 3-5 takes per concept, review before moving on

Audio

  • Test for background noise before shooting
  • Use backup audio recorder
  • Captions required for all platforms
  • Natural pacing - pauses are powerful

Wardrobe & Set

  • What they'd actually wear in the salon
  • Products visible and accessible on set
  • Salon sink available for wet hair demo (Video 4)
  • Old photos for B-roll if available (Video 5)

Export Specs

  • Vertical: 1080x1920 (TikTok/Reels)
  • Square: 1080x1080 (feed posts)
  • Landscape: 1920x1080 (YouTube/website)
  • Always include captions/subtitles

Content Rules

No medical claims - "helps support" not "treats/cures/heals"
No stat-dumping - save numbers for sales contexts
"Complimentary" not "free" in all external content
First names only for Tina and Jade
ALL credentials belong to Tina (Stylist, Trichologist, 40+ years)
No biotin or niacinamide claims (roadmap only, not in current formula)
AWAREBIO™ Complex ingredients: Tahitian microalgae, dual-weight HA, vegan keratin amino acids, organic green tea, organic turmeric, organic beet root
Talking points, not word-for-word scripts. Authenticity over perfection.
