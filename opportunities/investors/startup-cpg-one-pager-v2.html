Company Description
AWARE Hair is the first professional-exclusive scalp care system, created by Tina Mui, a master trichologist with 40+ years building scalp care programs for the Ritz Carlton, Fairmont, and Peninsula hotels. AWARE combines patent-pending bioactives with salon credibility to deliver clinically-backed results through a distribution model that turns every stylist into a scalp care consultant. No professional-exclusive scalp brand exists at scale today.
