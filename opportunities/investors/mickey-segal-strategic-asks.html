Retail & Editorial
1
Nordstrom
Expanding clean beauty and scalp wellness categories. Consultation-based beauty model fits AWARE's education-led sell. LA footprint allows a regional pilot before national rollout.
Pathway: Through your network - a warm intro to Nordstrom's beauty buying team carries weight a cold submission cannot.
The Ask: Warm intro to the beauty category buyer for a pilot in 3-5 LA-area stores.
2
Bluemercury
Luxury beauty retailer with integrated spa services (Macy's owned). Curates emerging clinical-wellness brands. Spa integration creates a natural entry point for scalp care.
Pathway: Through your network - Bluemercury's corporate parent (Macy's) is accessible via executive-level introductions.
The Ask: Intro to the brand curation team for a discovery meeting or pilot placement.
3
Bloomingdale's
Prestige beauty floor, appointment-based consultations. Scalp care is an open category on their floor. Their clientele expects exclusive, high-performance products.
Pathway: Through your network - even a second-degree introduction to their prestige beauty buyer changes the conversation.
The Ask: Intro to the prestige beauty buyer to present AWARE's salon-validated performance data.
4
Goop
High-reach wellness editorial platform. One feature creates leverage across retail and press conversations. Note: Goop sells competing scalp products, so this is an editorial play only, not distribution.
Pathway: Through your network - Goop's editorial team is embedded in the LA entertainment-wellness world you operate in.
The Ask: Intro for a product review or roundup feature - framed as editorial discovery, not a retail pitch.
5
Allure
Conde Nast beauty editorial. A Best of Beauty award or editor's pick is a force multiplier for retail, investor, and partnership conversations. Trichologist founder story fits their editorial format.
Pathway: Through your network - Conde Nast editors are reachable through entertainment and media circles you're connected to.
The Ask: Intro to beauty editors for product review consideration - AWARE's trichologist founder story is exactly what they feature.
