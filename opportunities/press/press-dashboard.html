Recommended Outreach Order
  • 1 Start with Tier 3 - Indie beauty specialists and trade press are more accessible and build credibility for bigger pitches
  • 2 Layer in Tier 4 - Podcasts and newsletters have longer lead times, start pitching early
  • 3 Then Tier 2 - Mid-tier consumer beauty editors, with Tier 3 coverage as social proof
  • 4 Finally Tier 1 - Major nationals (Allure, Vogue) once you have coverage clips to reference
Outreach Best Practices
  • * Send pitches Tue-Thu, 9-11am in the editor's timezone
  • * Personalize every pitch - reference their recent work
  • * Keep initial emails under 200 words
  • * Always offer samples with no obligation
  • * Never pitch exclusively to competing publications simultaneously
