Interactive HTML Dashboard

SKU Defaults

Formula Explanations

  • Reorder Point = (Avg Daily Sales x Lead Time) + Safety Stock
  • Safety Stock = (Max Daily Sales x Max Lead Time) - (Avg Daily Sales x Avg Lead Time)
  • Max Daily Sales = Avg Daily Sales x 1.5 (demand spike factor)
  • Reorder Quantity = 45 days of supply + safety stock buffer

Default Parameters

  • Lead time: 37 days
  • Lead time variability: +/- 10 days
  • Demand spike factor: 1.5x
  • Reorder supply target: 45 days

Scaling Projections

The model includes a salon count slider (50-200). As you scale, it recalculates safety stock, reorder points, reorder quantities, and capital required. At 100 salons, safety stock capital roughly doubles.

Needs Real Data

Default weekly sales and stock levels are estimates. Replace with actual Shopify sell-through data and current inventory counts to make the model accurate.
