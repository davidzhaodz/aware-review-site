Executive Summary

Partnership Objective

  • Industry-sponsored scalp microbiome clinical trial at USC Keck School of Medicine
  • Randomized, double-blind study with diverse hair types and scalp conditions
  • Goal: independent clinical validation of AWARE Hair's scalp care formulations

Key Contacts

  • Primary PI: Dr. Nada Elbuluk - alopecia researcher, Skin of Color Center founder, 90+ publications
  • Alternate PI: Dr. Jennifer Hsiao - 187+ publications, clinical trials infrastructure
  • Entry point: MESH Strategic Partnerships (mesh@usc.edu)

Institutional Pathway

  • Step 1 - MESH: Industry partnership intake. MESH matches us with faculty and frames the collaboration. This is the front door, not the trial office itself.
  • Step 2 - PI engagement: Work directly with matched faculty on study design and protocol.
  • Step 3 - CTO: Clinical Trials Office handles budget negotiation, contracts, and post-award management. This is where the trial formally gets set up.
  • Step 4 - IRB: HRPP reviews for regulatory/human subjects approval.

Document Overview

  • 11 faculty contacts across 3 priority tiers
  • 6 administrative offices with verified contact info
  • 5-phase outreach strategy with timeline
  • All contacts independently verified via USC sources
Bottom line: Dr. Nada Elbuluk is the strongest PI candidate - her alopecia and skin-of-color research directly overlaps with our study design, and her Skin of Color Center provides infrastructure for diverse participant recruitment. The recommended entry path is through MESH Strategic Partnerships (industry intake and faculty matching), with a parallel inquiry to the Dermatology Clinical Research team. MESH and the Clinical Trials Office (CTO) are separate offices - MESH gets us in the door and connected with the right PI, then CTO handles the budget, contracts, and trial administration once a faculty partner is identified.

Part 3 - Execution Plan

Recommended Outreach Strategy

Five-phase plan from initial inquiry through study execution.

Phase 1
Initial Partnership Inquiry
Week 1-2
  • Email MESH (Vasiliki Anest / mesh@usc.edu) with partnership overview, study scope, budget range ($100-170K)
  • Parallel email to skinstudy@med.usc.edu requesting information on faculty research alignment
  • Subject line: "Industry Partnership Inquiry - Scalp Microbiome Clinical Trial"
Phase 2
Faculty Introduction
Week 3-4
  • Request introduction to Dr. Nada Elbuluk (cite alignment: alopecia research, Skin of Color Center)
  • If unavailable, ask about Dr. Jennifer Hsiao as alternative
  • Prepare 1-page study concept summary for faculty meeting
Phase 3
Study Design Discussion
Week 5-8
  • Share full proposal with identified PI
  • Discuss design decisions: vehicle control, shotgun metagenomics, blinding protocol
  • PI refines protocol and provides budget input
  • Engage CTU (ctu@sc-ctsi.org) for infrastructure assessment if needed
Phase 4
Administrative Setup
Week 9-16
  • CTO budget/contract negotiation (Sara Katrdzhyan)
  • IRB submission through iStar
  • OnCore registration for trial management
  • Medicare Coverage Analysis if applicable
Phase 5
Execution
Week 16+
  • Study launch through department coordination
  • Ongoing CTO support for post-award management
