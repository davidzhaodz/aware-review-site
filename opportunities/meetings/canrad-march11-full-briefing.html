Pricing Strategy Options

Option A
Accept $13.75

Accept their ask for strategic value: Canada market entry, 10K+ salon network, proof of distribution. Very thin margin at 34% - viable only if volume ramps quickly. Risk: sets a low price floor globally.

Option B - Recommended
Counter at $16.50 with Graduated Pricing

Start at $16.50. Drop to $15 at $50K annual, $13.75 at $100K+. Rewards volume, protects margin at launch. Signals confidence. Gives CanRad a clear path to their number without giving it away upfront. Both sides have skin in the game.

Option C
Meet at $15

Practical middle ground. 40% AWARE margin, 45% CanRad margin. Simpler to negotiate than graduated structure. Appropriate if they push back hard on the tiered approach.

Discovery kit note: CanRad flagged the $75 discovery kit as "uncharted territory." Price it aggressively in the distribution agreement - it drives full-line conversion and ultimately increases CanRad's basket size. Frame it as a customer acquisition tool, not a standalone margin line.

Canadian Regulatory - Health Canada
Good news headline: No zinc pyrithione (ZnP) in AWARE formulas = cosmetic classification. No Natural Health Product (NHP) license required. The regulatory path is relatively simple.

CNF - Cosmetic Notification Form

What It Is

  • Post-market filing - no pre-approval needed
  • File within 10 days of first sale in Canada
  • Free to submit
  • Issues a Cosmetic Number (CN) needed for import documents
  • 9 sections including full INCI with concentrations

March 2025 Change

Now requires a Canadian address as Responsible Person. CanRad can serve this role as the Canadian importer. This is standard for international brands entering Canada through a distributor. Should be addressed in the distribution agreement.
