Canada Market Entry - Full Regulatory Detail

The One-Liner (Memorize This)

"We're prepared for Canadian market entry. Our formulas are already EU-compliant, which exceeds Health Canada requirements. We'd handle bilingual sticker labeling on our end before shipping, and we'd provide CUSMA Certification of Origin documentation so your imports are duty-free. We'd want CanRad to serve as the Canadian Responsible Person for Health Canada notification - that's standard practice in these partnerships."

Health Canada Regulatory

Process

  • Notification, not approval. Submit Cosmetic Notification Form (CNF) within 10 days of first Canadian sale.
  • No fee, no wait period.
  • As of March 2025, requires a Canadian address - CanRad serves as "Responsible Person."

Formula Compliance

  • AWARE is EU-compliant (stricter than Health Canada)
  • Must check against Health Canada Hotlist (hundreds of restricted/prohibited substances)
  • Consultant review: $500-1,500
  • Likely compliant given EU compliance

Bilingual Labeling

  • All mandatory elements in English AND French (product name, net quantity, warnings, directions)
  • Sticker labels are standard industry practice for market entry (not explicitly codified in statute, but widely accepted)
  • INCI ingredient names don't need translation (exception: scheduled ingredients like water must appear as "water/eau")
  • Quebec (Bill 96): French must be equal or greater prominence than English
  • Fragrance allergen disclosure: Phase 1 (24 allergens, April 2026) and Phase 2 (81 allergens, August 2026)

Trade & Tariffs - Deep Dive

Good news: Canada's retaliatory counter-tariffs on US cosmetics were rolled back in September 2025. CUSMA remains in full effect for US-manufactured goods. AWARE ships duty-free to Canada with proper documentation.

Current Tariff Status (Feb 2026)

  • CUSMA/USMCA duty: 0% for HS 3305.10 and 3305.90 (shampoo, conditioner, leave-in)
  • MFN rate without CUSMA docs: 6.5% - this is what you pay if documentation is incomplete
  • Canada counter-tariffs: Rolled back Sept 2025. Cosmetics were not in initial lists regardless.
  • IEEPA tariffs (US-side): Apply to Canadian goods entering US, not US goods entering Canada. Does not affect AWARE's exports to CanRad.

CUSMA Certification Requirements

No prescribed form - can be on commercial invoice. Must include 9 required data elements:

  • Certifier name, title, contact info
  • Exporter/producer identity
  • HS code + product description
  • Origin criterion (wholly produced or qualifies under rules of origin)
  • Blanket period (can cover 12 months of shipments)
  • Authorized signature and date

AWARE can self-certify. No third-party verification needed. One blanket cert covers a full year of orders.
