Core Narrative Frames
Channel Protection
"We looked at your full portfolio. Nioxin is at Shoppers for half-price. Schwarzkopf deliberately went to Amazon. Even Oligo, which says 'salon-only,' is on Amazon. We enforce MSRP with zero exceptions. No Amazon. No Shoppers. No mass retail. When your salons carry AWARE, their clients can only buy it from them."
Category Gap
"You carry 60+ brands including Nioxin and Zenagen - but those treat thinning and hair loss. Olaplex is bond repair. Nobody owns the proactive scalp wellness ritual. AWARE is the daily scalp care routine - it's the category your salons aren't serving yet."
Sell-Through, Not Shelf Price
"The question isn't whether $55 is expensive. The question is whether it sells and reorders. 96% of our B2B partners reorder within 2 months. Zero returns. 52% average sell-through rate. The price works because the product works."
Revenue Per Square Foot
"A salon carrying the 3-SKU ritual generates ~$170 retail per set sold. At 50% margin, that's $85 per client. How many of your current back-bar brands deliver that per transaction?"
Education as Pull
"We don't just ship product. Tina does scalp consultations and co-develops education with each salon. That creates client demand that drives sell-through - your consultants aren't pushing product, they're fulfilling demand."
Why Independence Matters
"We specifically chose to start with an independent. SalonCentric is L'Oreal. CosmoProf is Sally Beauty. Both have structural reasons to prioritize their parent brands. When CanRad recommends AWARE, it's because the brand is right for your salons, not because a parent company told you to push it."
Pilot Selection
"We're not looking for your 50 biggest accounts - we want the 50 most likely to activate. Salons with scalp on the menu, Biotop buyers, consistent retail reorderers, Green Circle certified. Your consultants' gut: which owners actually come to HairFest? Transactional accounts don't launch brands."
Proactive vs. Reactive (Category Label)
"Your scalp portfolio is excellent for treatment - Nioxin for thinning, Zenagen for loss, Biotop for active problems. What you don't have is the daily wellness ritual for the 90% of clients who don't have a problem yet. Same shift skincare made: from acne treatment to daily SPF and serums."
If Asked About Broader Canada
"Our strategy is depth before breadth. We want to prove the model in Ontario and Manitoba with the right partner before expanding nationally. If this works the way we expect, we'd want CanRad to be part of that national conversation."
Turnkey Salon Onboarding
"We have 4 opening packages from $623 to $2,801 - each includes complimentary backbar, displays, merchandising, and education. This isn't 'here's product, good luck.' It's a turnkey launch program with white-glove support."
Scalp Specialist Certification

Service Revenue Angle
