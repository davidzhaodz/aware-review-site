"Six months: $125K revenue, 50+ salon partners, 96% repurchase, zero returns. 18M organic social views. Press in LA Times, Beauty Independent, NewBeauty. Early-stage with proven product-market fit - and you'd be building the retail playbook with us, not inheriting someone else's."

"No brand in your portfolio is 100% scalp-focused with patent-pending actives. Kat Burki's hair line is a skincare extension. Oribe's Serene Scalp is a sub-line. AWARE is the only brand built entirely around scalp wellness by a practicing trichologist. We complement, not compete."
"What about Amazon?"
"Never on Amazon. Never will be. Full price protection guaranteed. Every other haircare brand in your portfolio is on Amazon - we're the one brand your retail partners can carry without online price competition."

"We supply 50+ salon partners with zero stockouts. LA-based manufacturing, 4-6 week lead times. For a retail launch, we'd pre-build inventory and forecast collaboratively. We'd want to discuss volume expectations to plan production."
