The AWARE Vision - Three Acts
Act 1 - The Foundation (Now)
Salon-Exclusive Scalp Care with Proven PMF
Product-market fit proven by 96% repurchase, 50+ salons, $0 paid spend. The only brand built FOR stylists BY a stylist. Clean formulations, professional-exclusive distribution. Stylists are the trusted prescribers - we arm them with products that work and education that builds authority. $125K in 6 months, all organic.
Act 2 - The Platform (Years 2-5)
AI-Enabled Scalp Wellness Network
AI scalp cameras in every partner salon (we eat the hardware cost - Oura model). "Scalp Wellness Certified Salon" program creates a branded network. Consumer-facing scalp tracking app (before/after progress photos, product recommendations, environment alerts). Service protocols, not just products - AWARE becomes the platform salons run their scalp services on.
Act 3 - The Ecosystem (Years 5-10)
The Scalp Wellness Brand
Medical referral network: hair restoration clinics ($7.4B market, 16.6% CAGR) send patients to AWARE-certified salons for ongoing care. Proprietary scalp health dataset - the largest longitudinal scalp data in the industry. Subscription model: product + service + tracking. Platform multiples (8-15x) instead of CPG (3-5x).

Don't formally pitch or present slides. This is peer-to-peer, not a pitch meeting.
