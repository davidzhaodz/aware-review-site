Section 09 Contract Clause Reference

Every relevant clause from the Original Agreement, quoted with context and relevance to the current negotiation.

Section 2.3 - Materials and Ingredients

"Manufacturer shall be responsible for procuring all materials and ingredients necessary to manufacture the Products, except for active ingredients (SO-Resisthyal-25 and SymHair Force 1631) for the first 10,000 bottles, which will be provided by the Company."
Relevance: Establishes fragrance as manufacturer's responsibility under original terms. AWARE is conceding this point going forward.

Section 5.1 - Price

Lists original per-unit prices for all products.
Relevance: These are the baseline prices being replaced by the new pricing.

Section 5.2 - Payment Terms

"Initial Payment: 30% of the total invoice price for retail-sized products ($27,720.00) and 50% of the total invoice price for bulk-sized products ($1,667) will be paid upfront before the initial production run."
Relevance: Establishes the deposit structure and installment schedule.

Section 5.3 - Lab Fee Credit

"The $3500 lab fee paid to the former lab (Adonis Inc.) shall be kept as a credit towards future development of new products or formulas."
Relevance: This credit is for R&D/formulation development only, not production costs. Not included in the production credit calculation.

Section 5.4 - Price Adjustments

"Manufacturer agrees that any price adjustments for future orders shall be subject to mutual agreement. Company expects price decreases for increased order volumes and long-term commitments."
Relevance: Price changes require mutual agreement - Boom Lab cannot unilaterally increase prices. Contract explicitly anticipates DECREASES with volume.

Section 6.1 - Term

"This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of one (1) year."
Relevance: Original 1-year term expired ~July 22, 2025. Both parties continued in good faith. The Amendment (Article 9) formally renews and extends through March 25, 2027 or completion of all production, whichever is later.

Section 13.3 - Entire Agreement

"This Agreement, including all exhibits and attachments hereto, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements, understandings, and representations, whether oral or written."
Relevance: The signed agreement supersedes any prior PIs, emails, or verbal agreements.

Section 13.4 - Amendments

"This Agreement may be amended or modified only by a written agreement signed by both parties."
Relevance: Any changes (including price increases) must be in writing and signed.

Section 4.6 - Penalties for Delayed Delivery

"If Manufacturer fails to deliver the Products within the agreed lead time, Manufacturer shall pay Company liquidated damages in the amount of 1% of the invoice value for each day of delay, up to a maximum of 50% of the total invoice value."
Relevance: AWARE has not enforced late delivery penalties but could. This is unused leverage.

Section 11.2 - Liability Cap

"The total cumulative liability of the Manufacturer arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall not be capped and shall cover all costs, damages, and losses incurred by the Company due to the Manufacturer's breach, negligence, or misconduct."
Relevance: Manufacturer liability is UNCAPPED - unusual and favorable to AWARE.

Section 12 - Force Majeure

Permits delay or termination only. Does NOT permit price changes.
Relevance: Rising material costs are not force majeure and do not entitle Boom Lab to unilateral price increases.
