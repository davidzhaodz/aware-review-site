Technical Health

The Lighthouse 0/100 score is misleading. Root cause: 8 homepage videos prevent LCP measurement. Without throttling, FCP is 2.2s and TBT is 0ms - both good. The real issues are video weight (9MB) and third-party script bloat (1.9MB).

Why Lighthouse Shows 0 But PageSpeed Looks Better

The homepage has 8 embedded videos (3 HD-1080p, 1 HD-720p, 4 SD-480p) totaling ~9MB. The hero video is the LCP element, but Lighthouse can't measure it because the video never fully loads in headless mode. When LCP returns null, the score collapses to 0. PageSpeed Insights uses real Chrome user data (CrUX) where users interact and videos load normally, so scores are higher.

  • The scalp quiz popup is NOT the problem - it's ~3-4KB, loads async, well-built
  • Without throttling: FCP 2.2s (good), TBT 0ms (excellent), TTI 2.2s (excellent)
  • The real fix: lazy-load below-fold videos + audit third-party scripts

Prioritized Recommendations

Ranked by impact-to-effort ratio. Quick wins first, then medium-term plays, then strategic investments. All aligned with the April 2026 pivot (salon engine, head spa, trich network, TikTok seeding).

This Week

1. Rewrite "9 in 1 Shampoo" Meta Title & Description
High Impact
The product page /products/aware-9-1-shampoo has 22,242 impressions at position 4.5 with only 1.1% CTR. The current meta title likely doesn't match search intent. Rewrite to emphasize what makes this a true 9-in-1 (benefits, not just the name). Add social proof (48 reviews, 5.0 rating) to the meta description. Target: 3-5% CTR = 600-1,100 clicks/quarter.
Effort: 30 minutes / Impact: +400-800 clicks/quarter
2. Optimize Blog Post Titles for CTR
High Impact
Two blog posts have strong impressions but terrible CTR: the tariff article (4,217 imp, 0.5% CTR) and the salon trends report (3,060 imp, 0.4% CTR). Rewrite meta titles to be more specific and benefit-driven. The tariff article title should mention the year and specific impact. The trends report should include a number ("7 Salon Trends Shaping 2026").
Effort: 1 hour / Impact: +50-100 clicks/quarter
3. Fix Descriptive Link Text (10 links)
Medium Impact
Lighthouse flagged 10 links without descriptive text. These are likely "Read more" or icon-only links. Replace with specific anchor text that describes the destination. This directly impacts the SEO score and helps Google understand page relationships.
Effort: 30 minutes / Impact: SEO score improvement

This Month

4. Optimize All Product Page Meta Descriptions
High Impact
Product pages collectively have 33,217 impressions but only 1.1% CTR. The leave-in (2,837 imp, 0.9%), conditioner (2,638 imp, 0.3%), and scalp care shampoo (2,434 imp, 1.3%) all need attention. Include key differentiators: "Professional scalp care", "9 benefits in 1", star rating, and a compelling value proposition in each meta description.
Effort: 2 hours / Impact: +100-300 clicks/quarter
5. Create "Scalp Gua Sha" Blog Content
Medium Impact
"Scalp gua sha" is a new query (not in previous period) with 65 impressions at position 4.6. AWARE sells a sandalwood gua sha - this is a perfect content-to-product pipeline. Write a comprehensive guide: "How to Use a Scalp Gua Sha for Hair Growth" targeting this query plus related long-tails. Aligns with the head spa / trichologist network strategy.
Effort: 3-4 hours / Impact: New content pipeline + product discovery
6. Populate Product FAQ & HowTo Schema
Medium Impact
Product FAQ and HowTo schemas are present in the theme but empty (metafields unpopulated). Fill these for all products. This enables rich results in Google (FAQ dropdowns, how-to steps) and provides structured data for AI search engines. Prioritize the shampoo page given its impression volume.
Effort: 2-3 hours / Impact: Rich results + AEO improvement
