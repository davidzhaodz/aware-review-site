Built on 40 Years of Scalp Science

AWARE Hair is a professional-exclusive scalp care brand founded in 2025 in California. Every product in the 9·1 Professional collection was developed from a single conviction: that lasting hair health begins not at the strand, but at the scalp environment. Our slogan, "Proactive Scalp Wellness, Simplified," reflects both the philosophy and the daily ritual we built around it.

Where most brands address hair concerns after they appear, AWARE was designed for prevention. Gentle enough for daily use, precise enough for professional trust.

The Founder

Tina Mui spent over four decades working directly with clients in a professional salon setting before co-founding AWARE. A licensed cosmetologist and master trichologist, she observed a consistent pattern: clients came in with scalp concerns that their existing haircare routines were not designed to address. Conventional shampoos cleansed the strand but disrupted the scalp. Moisturizing conditioners hydrated the mid-lengths but ignored the root environment. Nothing was built with the scalp microbiome as the starting point.

That gap became the foundation for AWARE. Tina collaborated with her daughter and co-founder, Jade, and David Zhao to bring the formulas from salon concept to professional market. The AWAREBIO Complex, the proprietary active blend at the core of every product, encodes four decades of clinical observation into a daily ritual that salons can stand behind and clients can sustain at home.

Our Philosophy

The scalp is skin. It has a microbiome, a moisture barrier, and a sebum balance. Disrupting any of these does not just cause temporary irritation - it changes the environment in which every hair follicle grows. Most haircare is formulated around the strand and tolerates the scalp as a side effect. AWARE inverts that sequence.

We formulate scalp-first. Every ingredient decision begins with a question: does this support the scalp environment, or does it compromise it for cosmetic effect? Sulfates, synthetic fragrances, parabens, silicones, and other common haircare additives were eliminated not as marketing choices, but as formulation ones. What remains is a botanically grounded, clinically studied system for daily proactive care.

We pair that scientific rigor with a commitment to simplicity. The 9·1 ritual - shampoo, conditioner, leave-in - consolidates nine essential functions into three steps. That is what "Proactive Scalp Wellness, Simplified" means in practice.

The AWAREBIO Complex

The AWAREBIO Complex is AWARE's patent-pending proprietary formulation, developed to address the scalp environment from multiple angles simultaneously. It combines biofermented Tahitian microalgae with dual-weight hyaluronic acid, vegan keratin amino acids, and a suite of organic botanical actives including green tea extract, turmeric extract, and beet root extract. Each component was selected for its documented role in supporting scalp hydration, resilience, and the conditions that favor healthy hair growth.

The complex is the active core of every product in the 9·1 Professional collection. It is not a fragrance story or a packaging differentiator. It is the formulation itself.
