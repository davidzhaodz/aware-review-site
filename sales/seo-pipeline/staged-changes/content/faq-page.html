Scalp Wellness

How often should I use the products?

The 9·1 ritual is designed for daily use. The shampoo and conditioner are gentle enough for everyday cleansing without disrupting the scalp's moisture balance. The leave-in treatment can be applied daily to damp or dry hair with no rinse required. Your stylist may recommend a specific cadence based on your scalp environment.

What is the difference between proactive and reactive scalp care?

Most scalp products are reactive - formulated to address concerns only after they appear. AWARE takes a proactive approach: gentle, consistent daily care that helps maintain a healthier scalp environment over time, rather than intervening after imbalance has already developed. Think of it as a daily maintenance practice rather than a corrective treatment.
