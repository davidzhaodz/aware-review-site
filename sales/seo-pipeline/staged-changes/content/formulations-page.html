Our Formulations

Every ingredient in the 9·1 Professional collection was chosen deliberately. That means understanding what each component does at the scalp level, how it interacts with the other actives in the formula, and whether its long-term daily use supports or subtly undermines the scalp environment we are working to maintain.

What We Include and Why

The active core of every 9·1 product is the patent-pending AWAREBIO Complex: a proprietary blend of biofermented Tahitian microalgae, dual-weight hyaluronic acid, vegan keratin amino acids, organic green tea extract (EGCG), organic turmeric extract, and organic beet root extract. Each of these was selected for a documented role in scalp hydration, antioxidant protection, follicle support, or structural hair integrity.

Supporting the complex are a series of botanical actives that reinforce the formula's scalp-first intention. Rosemary extract supports scalp circulation. Argan oil delivers lightweight lipid nourishment to the scalp surface without heaviness or buildup. Tea tree contributes natural antimicrobial properties that help keep the scalp environment balanced without the harshness of synthetic preservatives.

What We Chose Not to Include

Formulating without certain ingredients is not a marketing position - it is a consequence of taking the scalp environment seriously. Here is what is absent from every product in the 9·1 collection, and the reasoning behind each exclusion.

  • We chose to exclude sulfates (SLS/SLES) because aggressive surfactants strip the scalp's natural sebum and disrupt the pH balance that the rest of our actives work to maintain.
  • We chose to exclude silicones because they create a film that can accumulate at the scalp over time, interfering with absorption and follicle function.
  • We chose to exclude parabens and phthalates because cleaner preservation approaches exist and there is no reason to introduce unnecessary synthetic compounds into a daily scalp ritual.
  • We chose to exclude synthetic fragrances and dyes because they are among the most common contact sensitizers in haircare, and a formula designed for daily scalp use should not carry that risk. Our fragrance is 100% natural (Pacific Air).

Small-Batch Production

The 9·1 Professional collection is manufactured in small batches in a GMP-certified laboratory in California, formulated to EU cosmetic standards. Small-batch production is not a premium affectation. It ensures tighter quality control at every stage, fresher active concentrations per batch, and the ability to make precise adjustments as our formulation knowledge grows. It also means we do not produce excess inventory to hit volume minimums - every bottle that reaches a salon or a client was made to meet current demand.

Packaging is sustainably manufactured using recyclable food-grade plastic. The brand donates 1% of profits to scalp and dermatological wellness initiatives.
