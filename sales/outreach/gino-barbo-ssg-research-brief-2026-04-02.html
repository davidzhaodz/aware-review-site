Industry Intelligence

What's Happening in the Market Right Now

Olivia said Q1 was tough. She's right, and it's not just Nashville. Understanding these headwinds positions you as someone who gets the operator's reality, not a brand rep pitching in a vacuum.

The Salon Slowdown Is Real

  • Salon revenue contracted 1.0% in 2025 - the first annual decline after 5.5% post-COVID CAGR. First contraction since the pandemic recovery. Verified IBISWorld
  • New guest visits fell 9% YoY in 2024 per Zenoti data (30,000+ businesses). Existing guest visits grew only 1%, netting a 1% total decline. Verified
  • Clients stretching appointments from 6-8 weeks to 10-12 weeks, mirroring early 2007 recession patterns. NBC News, Bloomberg both reported this in April 2025. Verified
  • Consumer sentiment at 12-year low - University of Michigan index fell 11% in April 2025. Beauty foot traffic growth decelerated from 18% (2023) to 1.5% (2024). Verified

Tariffs Are Compressing Margins

  • 20% tariff on imported salon products from Italy, Germany, France. 10-34% on products from China. Color systems, tools, and accessories all affected. Verified Cosmetics Design
  • 51% of beauty businesses reporting late supplier payments due to cash flow pressure. Verified BCG
  • Hair extension salons facing $12-15K in annual cost increases. Distributors are raising prices, creating friction at the DSC-salon level. Verified

What this means for SSG: DSCs are walking into salons under budget pressure. They need products that create new revenue, not just add to the reorder list. A brand that comes with a business case ("add head spa services, generate $2-8K/month") is fundamentally different from a brand that says "add these 3 SKUs to your shelf."
